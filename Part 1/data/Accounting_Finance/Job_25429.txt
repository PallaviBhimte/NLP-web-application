Ideally you will have experience at a junior level within an EB consultancy or the EB arm of an IFA. You are likley to have already achieved level 3 and have made some progress towards or have already achieved diploma level. It could be that your path to an account manager role is blocked where you are and this could be your opportunity to step up a level within a short space of time.
