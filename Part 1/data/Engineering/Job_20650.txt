Description: Location Midlands only Salary Circa **** Closing date ****/****/2013 ****:**** Job Description There’s no telling how far your career could go with Jaguar Land Rover. Driven by inspiration and creativity, we’re painting a remarkable picture of success  expanding at an astounding rate and pushing forward into key markets worldwide. You’ll be at the very heart of our global success story – and you’ll enjoy an unrivalled opportunity to shape the latest developments both in the UK and overseas. As our global customer base grows, it’s never been more important to produce nearer to our most important new markets. Our exciting strategy for the years ahead recognises the crucial role joint ventures and international sourcing, engineering and development will play. Joining a dedicated International Manufacturing Products team in this vital post, you’ll underpin the creation of new overseas assembly plant delivery plans, plus supporting structures. What’s more, your role will extend to execution planning for new manufacturing sites, facilities and associated processes. Providing essential support and expertise, your responsibilities will span the creation of the project delivery plan, the development of process required for plan execution, facilitation of cross business project status reviews, and the creation of a high level launch plan. You’ll develop a range of key process and review inputs, support labour resource planning, and help to coordinate tracking project expenditure. Naturally, you’ll work closely with a broad range of specialist functions – from Manufacturing and Product Development to Corporate Real Estate, Legal and Purchasing, as well as external consultants. Candidate profile A driven individual with proven, relevant experience in international environments, you have a flexible approach to location, and the ability to travel freely throughout the UK and overseas. Ideally, you’ll have a strong background in manufacturing engineering or product development, with experience of launching a major product into production. Naturally, facility/plant layout experience will be a significant advantage. Experience of project management across facility installation is preferred, with a proven flair for commissioning new equipment and processes. We expect you to be qualified to degree level or equivalent. Our customers expect the best, and so do we, so you’ll be challenged every day. You’ll hone your skills to the leading edge and you’ll enjoy all the rewards that come from powering the growth of a prestigious luxury brand. So wherever you want to go in your career, you can be sure that Jaguar Land Rover is your ultimate destination. Benefits Our employees have access to a privilege car purchase scheme, membership of a competitive pension plan, performance related bonus scheme and 26 days holiday entitlement.
