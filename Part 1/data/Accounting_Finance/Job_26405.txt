Title: Commercial Accounting Manager

Description: My client is an internationally successful organisation and a market leader in their industry sector who are currently recruiting for a commercial accounting manager to join their finance transaction team. You will be responsible for managing a team of 510 people and ensuring all the administration of the financial transactions associated with their customers are completed accurately and on time. You will coach and provide support to team members whilst monitoring and enforcing the teams KPI's and targets. You will be responsible for improving processes and procedures, resolving complex queries, audit checking as well as adhoc project work The successful applicant will have gained at least 2 years experience in a similar role where you will have managed or supervised a team, you will also be able to demonstrate your ability to hold peers and managers accountable and provide evidence where you have done so previously. You will have previous transactional accounting experience ideally gained in a large corporate. You will be intermediate to advanced excel and SAP experience would be an advantage. You will enjoy working in a fast paced and dynamic working environment which enables you to add value and make a difference in your work.
