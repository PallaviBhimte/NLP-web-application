Title: Chef de Partie – Packhorse, food led pub
Webindex: 67772759
Company: Home Counties Pub Restaurants
Description: We are Home Counties Pub Restaurants, a branch of the awardwinning Brunning and Price family of pubs. We have reviewed everything from scratch and are currently developing new menus, concentrating on offering good, fresh dishes in a simple style with an emphasis on quality ingredients. We’re looking for an ambitious and committed chef with a passion for food who is ready to take the next step in his or her career. For us it’s all about serving fresh, simple, high quality traditional dishes extremely well. This is a busy working environment, and as well as having high standards and demonstrating good attention to detail, you’ll help the crew set the bar high in terms of delicious dishes, effective kitchen controls and excellent standards of health and hygiene. We see our CDP’s as being on the first step of the ladder to a senior chef role and we’ll provide support and development opportunities for you along the way. We have a website at www.packhorse.hcpr.co.uk but it’s also worth having a good look through the Brunning and Price website at www.brunningandprice.co.uk to get a feel of where we're heading .
