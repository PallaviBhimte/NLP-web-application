Title: HOTEL RECEPTIONIST
Webindex: 67237106
Company: Honiley Court Hotel & Conference Centre
Description: The receptionist is the first point of customer contact so must be very well presented, with neat conservative grooming and good social skills. Duties include answering the switchboard, taking telephonic or computerised reservations, welcoming guests to the hotel and allocating them suitable rooms, taking payments by cash or credit card. Responsibilities include balancing opening and closing shift cash floats and accounts, posting guests charges onto their rooms accounts. Shifts are by rotation and start either at 07.00am till 3.00pm or 3.00pm till 11.00pm, working 5 days out of 7 and including week ends, also by rotation.
