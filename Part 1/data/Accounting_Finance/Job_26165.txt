Title: Direct Debit Administrator
Webindex: 69064455
Company: Royal Air Forces Association
Description: The RAF Association is a membership organisation with charitable objectives with its Head Office based in Leicester. The Direct Debit Administrator is responsible for assisting with the collection of membership subscriptions and the maintenance of membership information on our database. You will also be responsible for processing fundraising income. We are looking for someone with previous experience in an accounts department of a small to medium sized organisation who can process large volumes of data accurately and has experience of direct debit collecting methods. You will need to be able to input large volumes of transactions and have a good telephone manner. If you are interested in this position please go to our website for further details and an application form which will need to be submitted before Thursday 7th February 2013. Please be aware we do NOT accept CVs. We recruit ourselves so no agencies please or organisations looking to place apprentices.
