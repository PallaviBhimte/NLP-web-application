Description: An opportunity has arisen to join an international group based in West Yorkshire in the role of Group Transactions Manager. Essentially you will be a Manager or Team Leader in one or more of the above disciplines. Exposure to working within a shared service environment would be an advantage as would be managing invoices with a ranging value from low to high and in a high volume environment. This is a great chance to develop and lead a highly capable transactions team and make a notable impact in a business that is well know for rewarding talent.
