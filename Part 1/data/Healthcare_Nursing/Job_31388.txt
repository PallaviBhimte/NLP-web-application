Description: Site or Unit : Huntercombe Campsie House Centre Address : Canal Street Kirkintilloch near Glasgow G**** ****QY Contracted Weekly Hours : **** Campsie House Neurodisability Centre, part of the Huntercombe Group, is located in the town of Kirkintilloch eight miles east of Glasgow and is served well by public transport with buses coming in from Glasgow City Centre. We provide Nursing Care and personal support for **** residents with complex neurological conditions who have low, medium or high nursing dependency needs. We look to tailor our programmes specifically to meet the needs of each individual to enable them to meet personal goals and improve their quality of life. The service has full induction and further support is given through our supervision and development programme. As a Registered Nurse you will work closely with the Centre Manager and deputise in their absence. You will be responsible for carrying out general nursing responsibilities including the overall well being of the residents and assisting with the formulation and implementation of the delivery of excellent standards of care; promoting independence and dignity and positively enhancing the lives of individuals in our care. So as a Registered Nurse what do you need to do to make this happen? Support the Deputy Nurse Manager and Registered Manager with the day to day nursing responsibilities Carry out ongoing assessments to determine individual needs and assist with the formulation of personal care plans Work Closely with the residents to promote greater independence and encourage a wide range of activities Ensure high standards of personal health hygiene.
