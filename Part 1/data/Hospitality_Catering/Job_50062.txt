Title: Apprentice Junior Chef
Webindex: 69147806
Description: A public house with a growing reputation for excellent service and freshly prepared food is looking to recruit an enthusiastic Apprentice Junior Chef to assist the Head Chef. Your duties will include: assisting in the preparation and professional cooking of hot and cold food within the kitchen; assisting in settingup and closing the kitchen; ordering stock and wastage control; cleaning the equipment and the kitchen; ensuring high standards of hygiene and personal appearance. This is an excellent opportunity for a motivated individual with a passion for cookery to develop your skills and to gain experience working with an experienced team.
