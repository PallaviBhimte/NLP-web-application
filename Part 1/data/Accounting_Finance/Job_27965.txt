Title: Temporary Cash Allocations Clerk
Webindex: 70758159
Company: FRS Ltd
Description: The main purpose of this role is the reconciliation and management of the cash and cheques banked by half of the company s branches. This will be a one month contract. Working alongside another member of the team, your responsibilities will involve: o Investigating discrepancies between what the branches have reported on the system to what they have taken and what is physically credited through the bank. o Liaising with the branches via memos and telephone calls and also emails and phone calls to the bank on a regular basis. o Some manual matching on bank reconciliation system. o Reconciliation is mainly done on spreadsheets within Excel. o Helping the branches to close their overnight process. o To help out in other areas within the Cashiers Department, time permitting. o Ad hoc duties to cover Holidays and Sickness. Skills Required: Previous Accounts experience is essential. Good communication skills are essential. Good basic Excel Skills. Ability to work well on your own as well as within a team.
