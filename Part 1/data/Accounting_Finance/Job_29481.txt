Title: Senior Cost Analyst

Description: I have an urgent need for a Cost Analyst to join a business in the NE. Purpose of Job Processes / analyses costs of production and overheads to ensure the business is able to accurately reflect operating performance. Ensures costs incurred relating to inventory and production are recorded and controlled. Principal Accountabilities  Assisting in month end close of the general ledger  Analyzing actual manufacturing costs and preparing periodic reports  Developing inventory analysis reports and analyzing variances  Assisting in the improvement in internal controls related to inventory  Verifying accurate of costings  Conducts physical inventories and monitors cycle count program  Reconciling inventory accounts to the general ledger  Preparing monthly journal entries as needed  Recording cost information for use in controlling expenditures  Assisting in preparing management with reports specifying and comparing factors affecting prices and profitability of products or services.
