Title: Lead Electrical Machines Engineer
Webindex: 69555196

Description: Lead Electrical Machines Engineer Swindon **** This is a fantastic opportunity to work with a leading UK integrated energy company. Reporting to the Electrical Group Head, as a Lead Engineer you will have responsibility for technical and managerial aspects of the Electrical Machines Section. In this role you will be responsible for: Providing technical support to the fleet and the client s wider operating companies Specification of new electrical plant Generator Inspections Assist in fault investigations Ensure compliance with relevant standards Technical custodian of the client s Technical Procedures Managing workload/resources within the Electrical Machines Group Mentoring of staff The ideal candidate will be degree qualified (or equivalent) and have or be working towards Chartered Status with a professional institution. Technically you should have a good knowledge of the design and manufacture of generators, generator auxiliary systems and motors with enough experience to investigate faults and report to senior management. You will have an understanding of the operation of conventional and modern power stations and will be up to date with relevant standards associated with generator design and testing and the ability to interpret generator test results.
