Title: Assistant Management Accountant
Webindex: 63779933
Company: CMC Consulting Limited
Description: Our client is a prestigious retail company based in central London and they are currently looking for an Assistant Management Accountant to join their Finance team. The role will be responsible for producing regular reports and bank reconciliations as well as supporting the month end process and driving improvements to reporting, maintaining weekly cash flow forecasts, reviewing funds, analysis of balance sheet accounts, providing cover and support for the credit controller, preparing period expenses, and assisting in the production of monthly accounts. You will ideally be a part qualified accountant who is degree educated and has excellent technical accounting skills, strong leadership skills, and commercial awareness. The ideal candidate will also have previous experience in a similar role. You will possess a strong technical and reporting skill set and be comfortable using excel to an advanced level. You will be a confident communicator and have the ability to liaise with senior stakeholders and nonfinance personnel. Where UK qualifications are specified we will take into account overseas equivalents. To apply for this position candidates must be eligible to work in country in which the role is based.
