Description: This agency has continued to produce cutting edge campaigns throughout 2012. As a result they have won yet another huge account, which has resulted in the need for a new Senior Account Director. You will manage and develop a small account management team, working closely with the MD to nurture and get the most of your team. You will understand the client's business and develop an in depth understanding of how they work; implementing marketing schedules to ensure all marketing activity is completed to the agreed deadlines. This is well established retail agency based on the outskirts of Leeds so candidates MUST have worked on retail/consumer focused accounts. You will be commercially minded and be willing to focus your attention and strategic knowledge on one brand.
