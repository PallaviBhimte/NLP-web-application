Main Accountabilities / Competencies: Translate customer specifications and aspects of device design into a suitable bespoke device package. Drive the design, procurement and assessment of the package and associated jigs/equipment. Identify and solve unusual technical problems during the design stage. May require leading a group. Update package design guidelines. Able to work without supervision. Communicate technical information to both internal and external parties (Engineering Group Leader and Project Manager, suppliers and Customer). This information to be in the form of drawings, written comment and presentations where required. Take part in or lead exercises aimed at furthering the group knowledge and identifying the solution to technical issues associated with specific Imaging device Projects. Ensure that internal documentation is produced as required including process specifications, flow charts, device travellers, Risk/COSHH assessments. Provide support in this area to less experienced staff. Work with Imaging Applications and Line Engineers to define and provide investigative support to areas of future development. Appreciation of thermal and stress modelling techniques. Proven problem solving abilities and use of Company and external investigative facilities. Able to lead groups of Engineers / Technicians in development of complex bespoke package/process design would be an advantage. Build productive relationships. Perform under pressure. Able to learn quickly. Good communication skills in the form of sketches, drawings and in writing. Considerable relevant experience.
