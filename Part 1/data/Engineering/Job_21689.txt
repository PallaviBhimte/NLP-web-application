Title: Quality and Training Manager

Company: Sykes Co Recruitment
Description: Working for an Internationally renowned electronic component manufacturer the primary purpose of the role of Quality & Training Manager will be to manage, proactively develop and maintain the Company Quality Management System in accordance with regulatory and business requirements. Furthermore the Quality & Training Manager shall seek to improve the efficiency and effectiveness of the Quality System to meet the business strategy of the Company. This position will also coordinate and where necessary provide all training in workmanship standards and operational standards as required by the business. This is an excellent role for a electronics specialist who enjoys working on more than one project at any one time and who thrives in a busy project driven environment
