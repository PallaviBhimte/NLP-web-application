Title: Support Technician
Webindex: 66053022
Company: Travail Emplyment Group
Description: We are currently looking for an Applications Support Technician for a Grantham based software development company. The purpose of this role is to provide first line technical support to customers, to assist the Support Manager in coordinating helpdesk activities to ensure service level targets are met or exceeded and to promote and develop the helpdesk function and software to improve customer satisfaction, job turnaround and internal communication. Duties within this role will include receiving telephone support calls/emails, diagnosing problems, writing SQL reports, testing software changes and deploying updates, whilst communicating with the customer at each point. To be considered for this role it is essential that you have an existing working knowledge of SQL. Part of the Interview process will be to sit an SQL test which has to be passed before processing to the next stage. Knowledge of the Delphi programming language would be a distinct advantage but is not a requirement as training can be provided. You should be a fast learner as you will need to quickly gain a full understanding of the operation of software products, including the customer's business processes they support. As a person you will be confident, outgoing with a friendly nature as the role requires a professional telephone manner. Unfortunately due to the high volume of applications we receive, we are unable to provide individual feedback. If you have not heard from us within 7 days, please assume that your application has not been successful.
