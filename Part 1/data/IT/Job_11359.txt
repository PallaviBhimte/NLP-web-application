Title: SAP PP/MM Consultant  Manufacturing Industry
Webindex: 71566330
Company: Consulting Point
Description: Job Description The successful candidate will be a SAP Production Planning (PP) Consultant capable of designing, building, testing and providing postimplementation support of shopfloor production processes and therefore experienced in the implementation of Production Planning (PP) and Materials Management (MM) components of SAP's ECC Business Application. They will have demonstrable experience of completing at least three (with one in a team lead role) full lifecycle SAP Production Planning implementations ideally in complex production environments such as that found in the Aerospace sector. They will have a good understanding of integration not just with other SAP components, such as Finance, but also third party partner products such as those supporting PDM, Shopfloor Data Collection (SFDC) and Workforce Scheduling business processes. Key Responsibilities Day to day activities include:  Analysis of legacy systems and business processes  Leading requirements gathering, design and gap analysis workshops to identify the optimum SAP solution  Leading the configuration of the SAP solution, including coordinating remote development  Application and use of best practice Production systems and processes, including MRPII, Lean **** Sigma  Architecting and authoring Production Planning solution content in support of sales activities. The required core skills include detailed knowledge of the SAP PP, MM and at least one of a relevant Industry Solutions, e.g. ISDIMP. A good understanding of how these solutions can be deployed through SAP's Enterprise Portal and integrate to other SAP Business Applications such as SRM and third party products through tools such as PI is also required.
