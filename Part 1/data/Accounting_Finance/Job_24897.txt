Title: Manager  Operational Tax
Webindex: 68679472
Company: Brewer Morris
Description: The main responsibilities will be to ensure full compliance with tax obligations (compliance, advisory, planning) regarding the identification, tax treatment and reporting of transactional and operational taxes, in particular the Foreign Account Tax Compliance Act (FATCA), Qualified Intermediary (QI) and Financial Transactions Tax (FTT) regimes. To assist in the development, monitoring and implementation of a Group Tax Risk Strategy for operational taxes, with focus on FATCA and FTT. The jobholder will have responsibility for dealings with key project stakeholders worldwide. In the application of tax treatment and the evaluation monitoring and control of tax risk, the jobholder will be guided by the relevant policies / procedures developed to comply with the relevant legislation and compliance/regulatory rules of the jurisdiction concerned with the assistance of the regional tax centres and the related operational businesses. You will need to have significant experience of implementing operational tax procedures with excellent working knowledge of FATCA. Please note our advertisements use PQE/salary levels purely as a guide. However we are happy to consider applications from all candidates who are able to demonstrate the skills necessary to fulfil the role.
