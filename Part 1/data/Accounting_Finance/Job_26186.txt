Title: Pension Communications Officer
Webindex: 69072581
Company: IPS Group
Description: This established and highly regarded inhouse pension department are looking to add a Pension Communications Officer to their existing Merseyside based team. Working alongside the technical department you will be responsible for communicating pension content to scheme members, institutions, trustees. You will write produce and review scheme communications and deliver presentations, both internally and externally, and take questions on the content of the presentations. You will have a solid understanding of occupational scheme administration, and sound technical knowledge of scheme legislation and regulation. You possess top class presenting skills and will have the ability to communicate on pension issues at all levels. Candidates who have been involved in delivering communications through a broad range of media, and who can help the team communicate more creatively and effectively will hold a distinct advantage.
