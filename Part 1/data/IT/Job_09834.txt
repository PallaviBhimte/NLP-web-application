Description: IT Support Analyst  Windows 7, Server **** AD A very longstanding and successful property business that is based in the heart of Central London has a requirement for an IT Support Analyst to join their small infrastructure and desktop support function. In this role the successful candidate will be providing a mixture of helpdesk, desktop and infrastructure support with approximately 60% of the role being user focused. The desktop and server infrastructure is comprised of mainstream Microsoft technologies, and within the server environment there is also some Linux, which may present a good learning opportunity for someone with an interest in this arena. To be suitable for this role, it is essential that you have previous experience in an IT Support role. As part of your experience you will need to have provided support to the server environment and be at a level where you can perform diagnostics and tuning, and create Group Policy Objects in Active Directory. Ideally, your experience will be underpinned by a Microsoft Server related qualification. In addition to your server experience, you must be familiar with mainstream Microsoft desktop applications and Operating systems such as MS Office and Windows XP/7. You will also need to be able to support PC hardware and peripherals. Exceptional written and spoken communication skills are a prerequisite. To apply for this role, please carefully detail all your relevant skills and experience in your CV. Where possible, please use reference ASH****TP in the Subject Line of your application.
