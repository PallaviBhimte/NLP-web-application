Title: Apprentice Fire Extinguisher Servicing Technician
Webindex: 70590906
Company: Target Fire Protection
Description: Working alongside a qualified technician, you will be trained on all aspects of Fire Extinguishers including installing, servicing, surveying and providing quotes. You will also be trained on installing Fire Alarms and PAT Testing, so an interest in Electrical Engineering is required. As part of the Apprenticeship you will learn a wide range of Engineering skills and also work towards industry recognised standards. Suitable applicants should ideally hold a full valid Driving Licence.
