Title: Support Worker – Essex Residential Services (Learning Disabilities)
Webindex: 70252009
Company: Cambian Group
Description: The Cambian Group, the UK’s largest provider of specialist services in education, mental health rehabilitation and learning disabilities, is looking for Support Workers to join our Essex Residential Services Team. We offer excellent training and career progression opportunities as well as a variety of staff benefits. Residential Services Residential Services provide a longer term placement for adults with learning disabilities, who require a supportive home with round the clock care. Job Role Successful candidates will play an active role in the day to day care and support for the client group, implementing care plans, creating a homely atmosphere, supporting activities and trips for patients, and assisting with supporting patients with general lifeskills. Candidate Profile If you have a passion for working with people in a person – centred way, and are energetic, able to work flexibly both under your own initiative as well as part of a team than this is the ideal role for you. Successful candidates will have to meet the person specification in order to be offered this post. Cambian is committed to Safeguarding and protecting the young people and service users within our care. All candidates will be subject to an enhanced DBS check and reference checks.
