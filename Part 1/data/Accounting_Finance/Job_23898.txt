Title: Senior Fund Accountant
Webindex: 68013859
Company: Walker Hamill
Description: Key responsibilities: This is an exciting, broad role within a growing business. You will be responsible for the preparation of the monthly reporting packs for presentation to the Board and for internal company presentations. You will also undertake the financial reporting of the investment funds and the preparation of the investment accounts. Moreover, you will prepare meaningful management information for Board level & internal purposes and provide financial analysis on the numbers. Essential requirements: The ideal candidate will be a qualified accountant (ACA, ACCA, CIMA or equiv.) with post qualification experience in private equity fund accounting. We may also consider practice trained ACAs who have audited private equity firms who have remained in practice after qualifying. You must possess a handson approach, excellent written & verbal communication skills and the ability to succeed in a fastpaced and complex environment with simultaneous reporting demands.
