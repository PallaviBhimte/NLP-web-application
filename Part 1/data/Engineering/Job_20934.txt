Title: Mechanical Engineering Maintenance Fitter
Webindex: 71675990
Company: Hays
Description: My client is company that designs, develops and manufactures a range of motors for use in missiles. The role will involve: Regular maintenance of mechanical plant, including compressors, pumps, fume extract systems, shot blasters and hydraulic systems. Chemical dosing of water based systems. Scheduled maintenance work associated with specialist machinery. Development of planned preventative maintenance schedules and keeping of computer based records. Installation, repair and both breakdown and planned preventative maintenance of mechanical engineering plant. The candidate should have Served a recognised apprenticeship in a mechanical engineering discipline or have attained an appropriate vocational / academic qualification. Experience of having been employed in a mechanical engineering maintenance role. Be self motivated / be able to work on your own or as part of a team. Be able to operate both lathe and milling machines. Qualifications in the use of fork lift trucks would be an advantage. Hold a current driving license.
