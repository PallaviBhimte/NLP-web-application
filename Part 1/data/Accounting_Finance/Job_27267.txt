Title: Financial Accountant / Management Accountant
Webindex: 69928360
Company: Goodman Masson
Description: A prestigious Media organisation based in the heart of the City require an accountant with a mixed financial and management accounting skillset who will report directly to the Head of Finance and will become an integral part of the Finance Team. Responsibilities include the production of statutory accounts, balance sheet reconciliations, P L and other ad hoc duties. Due to the nature of the role applicants must be confident in their own abilities to produce accounts with little or no supervision. The ideal candidate will be confident and articulate and able to interact well with both finance and nonfinance individuals. This is an excellent opportunity to work for part of a high profile company and requires an almost immediate start. Applicants must be accountancy qualified (ACCA/CIMA/ACA or equivalent) with prior experience in the Media sector and will have advanced Excel skills. Strong double entry accounting experience is also prerequisite to application. Goodman Masson is acting as an Employment Business in relation to this vacancy. Goodman Masson is an equal opportunities employer.
