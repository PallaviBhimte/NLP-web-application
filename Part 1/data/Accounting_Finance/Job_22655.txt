Title: Investor Relations Manager
Webindex: 61343060
Company: CMC Consulting Limited
Description: A listed natural business is recruiting an Investor Relations Manager to work in this high profile team. You will report to the Head of Investor Relations and collaborate with all areas of the business. You will demonstrate a strong understanding of the natural resources industry, the ability to address the concerns and issues of investors and prepare insightful materials ahead of IR events. You will be able to communicate effectively with analysts and investors and ensure a consistent message is communicated on behalf of this diverse group. You will take responsibility for the model and tracking of analysts forecasts, track trends in shareholder register and develop new reporting tools , improve quality of information provided to the management team and external stakeholders, and be up to date with industry research. The successful candidate will ideally possess very strong written communication and relationship building skills, strong knowledge of the financial and equity markets, and financial modelling and analysis skills gained from an analytical role in the natural resources industry. Where UK qualifications are specified we will take into account overseas equivalents. To apply for this position candidates must be eligible to work in country in which the role is based.
