Title: Qualified Social Workers

Company: Hi Flyers Recruitment
Description: Qualified Social Workers required in the Preston and surrounding areas. Qualified Social Workers needed for various long term temporary placements within the social work field. Roles include: Learning and Physical Disabilities, Adult Mental Health, Hospital Social Work, Older People's services, Adult Social Care and Children and Families. You must be a Qualified Social Worker and be GSCC/HCPC registered . The hours of work will vary so flexibility is essential. You will carry a caseload of clients within this position and you will be safeguarding clients also. An Enhanced CRB will also be required, an application can be provided that will need payment for, this will be reimbursed after 15 days placements.
