Title: Senior HR Business Partner, Banking / financial services
Webindex: 68696602
Company: Carr Lyons.
Description: Our client, a global financial services institution, has an exciting opportunity for a HR Business Partner to join them on a permanent basis. We are looking for an outstanding relationship manager who will influence and drive the people agenda within their client group, particular strengths and experience in change and talent management are essential. Responsibilities:  Influence and enable the effective diagnosis of business issues/ strategy and goals, turning these into People Plans  Take a proactive role in the development of HR Priorities and lead the implementation of these and other major change as required within business areas, turning them into real action plans.  Provide active leadership in conjunction with stakeholders on HR issues such as reward, employee relations, talent, performance, organisation development/change, culture, leadership and learning and development  Challenge, provoke and coach business leaders on how to engage employees and address resulting people issues and build people capabilities effectively. To drive the raising of the performance bar and build a strong and rigorous development culture.  Ensure that key HR operational processes are delivered successfully to the business, including ensuring that transactional HR processes delivered by the HR shared service to service levels  Provide leadership to cyclical annual processes including; performance development and annual payround process  Provide leadership to the recruitment process within client group and have personal involvement in senior hires, including reward package development  Take accountability for partnering with business leaders to ensure that all Regulatory and Legal requirements are met at all times. This is a fantastic career opportunity for a Senior HR Business Partner who loves change There is substantial business change, and a real opportunity to influence and drive the strategic people agenda around this. You will have to be an outstanding relationship manager, with a strong generalist background and a track record of delivering HR Business Partner support in large, corporate matrix businesses, ideally from a similar Banking / financial services background.
