Description: Social Worker  Looked after Children  West Midlands A Qualified Social Worker is required to join a busy Looked after Children Team in the Birmingham area. Working within the Looked after Children team, you will have the following responsibilities   To ensure that children placed in the care of the council have all their emotional, educational, social needs met.  Meet the statutory deadlines for placement reviews  Liaise with Foster Parents and Home Managers in ensuring placements are secure.  To investigate any concerns of neglect or abuse  Manage any legal duties involving court orders on behalf of the council  To work with children and their families in ensuring the family unit remains together where possible.  Manage your time and caseload with accurate timekeeping and information recording. Whilst the managers will welcome applications from a wide range of social workers, it is essential that you have knowledge of Child Protection. All appointments are subject to CRB clearances and HCPC registration.
