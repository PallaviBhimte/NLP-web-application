Title: Teaching Assistant for English Department
Webindex: 72242312
Company: Hays
Description: Level 3 Teaching Assistant job. Required to support pupils with EAL in the English department of a secondary school based in the north of Sheffield. This temporary position is to start as soon as possible and will continue on an ongoing basis. A Teaching Assistant is required to work with the English department supporting whole classes and taking withdrawal groups. Withdrawal groups will mainly consist of pupils with EAL, some pupils will have recently moved to the UK and have very limited levels of English. You will be required to work closely with these pupils to develop their English using Phonics and Basic Reading programmes. Previous experience of working with children with EAL and a primary background will be advantageous. You will need to work independently and be confident supporting individuals and groups of pupils; you will need to have strong behaviour management. The school are looking for a confident practitioner who can settle into this role quickly and work well with pupils. This position is open to Teaching Assistants who have gained at least a level 3 qualification, or have worked in an equivalent role; Teachers and Cover Supervisors with relevant experience are also welcomed to apply.
