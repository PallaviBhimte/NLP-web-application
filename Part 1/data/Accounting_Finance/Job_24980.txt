Title: Benefits Advisor

Company: Randstad Financial & Professional
Description: A fastpaced growing organisation is looking to expand its Wellbeing and Benefits team by recruiting a Benefits Advisor. You'll be responsible for handling the daytoday activities involved in managing the large benefits portfolio on offer. As part of this, you'll be responsible for being a subject matter expert to assist and advise people throughout the organisation on how the benefits can work for them. The role will be spread across two responsibilities of advising the business and it's employees of the range of benefits on offer including holding dropin centres and inductions and working to develop the current benefit package on offer. It is essential you have the following experience: experience in an employee benefits role working with a benefits platform in a busy environment dealing with flexible benefits and being the 'go to' person for benefits advice running and reconciling reports and presenting these to senior management being proficient with Excel (though you don't have to be an expert) subject matter expertise within benefits and being visible to the business as a benefits expert to be able to explain complex benefits policies to colleagues, comfortable running workshops and presenting to people building strong relationships with internal stakeholders and our external providers working in an extremely busy environment being able to juggle multiple tasks while also responding to people's needs for support and guidance prioritising to hit deadlines and manage own workload effectively This role is ideally suited to someone who has the above experience within a large organisation of **** employees. Confidence to immerse yourself into a business and be a point of contact is also key to the success of this role. Randstad Financial Professional encourage applications from individuals of all ages backgrounds.
