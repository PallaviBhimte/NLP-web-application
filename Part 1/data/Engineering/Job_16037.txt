Title: Integrity Engineer
Webindex: 68214393
Company: Team Recruitment  Ltd
Description:  Interpreting corrosion monitoring and inspection data in the database and recommending changes in the corrosion monitoring/inspection programmes.  Provide details and information to ensure annual corrosion monitoring work scopes and inspection plans are prepared.  Monitor and ensure weekly and monthly listing, such as overdue reports, are maintained.  Long term corrosion monitoring and inspection planning activities.  Plan and organise mobilisation requirements such as accommodations, flights and equipment associated with offshore work.  Progress inspection reports, repair orders etc. and update reports.  Manage the CTR (Cost, Time & Resources) process, and assist in budget preparation.  Support the Senior Discipline Engineers in the assessment of problems both technical and operational by sourcing information, progressing actions, notification of actions etc.  Perform relief Offshore Inspection Engineer duties as and when required.  Assist in the preparation of the annual IIM budgets for any assigned Assets.  Ensure compliance with the company and the Client, HSEQ policies and systems.  Undertake other duties as required by Senior Engineers. General  The list of the job duties is not exclusive or exhaustive and the post holder will be required to undertake tasks that may reasonably be expected within the scope of the post.  Assist with any other duties as and when required.  Undertake specific projects as instructed by management. Safety  Ensure that the safety policies and procedures are understood and adhered to so far as is reasonably practicable in order to ensure a safe working environment. Other  Carry out work in accordance with the Company Health, Safety, Environmental and Quality Systems.  Perform the assigned tasks with due diligence regarding the Integrated Management Systems on the Company.
