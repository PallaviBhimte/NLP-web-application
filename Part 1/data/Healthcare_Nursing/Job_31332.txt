Title: Field Care Supervisor, Bristol
Webindex: 61936400
Description: Allied Healthcare is one of the UK's leading providers of domiciliary care and healthcare staffing services. An opportunity has arisen for a Field Care Supervisor to join our team in Bristol. The successful applicant will be responsible for the ongoing field supervision of domiciliary and continuing healthcare support workers and will ensure that patient or service user requirements are met whilst maintaining compliance of care and nursing standards in accordance with AHG and Local Authority policies and procedures. You must demonstrate commitment to working within a team and have a flexible approach to working hours and practices. This role will involve an element of oncall duties. You must possess a minimum of NVQ Level 2 in Health and Social Care (NVQ Level 3 highly desirable) and will be working towards NVQ Level 3 or **** or equivalent.
