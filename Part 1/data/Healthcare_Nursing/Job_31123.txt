Description: Eden Brown Recruitment is looking for an Occupational Health Nurse Advisor to join a team responsible for the provision of high quality and efficient Occupational Health Nursing Services. Ideally you will have at least 3 years post registration Occupational Health experience and have a good knowledge of current OH legislation.
