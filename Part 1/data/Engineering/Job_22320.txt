Title: Building Structures Engineer
Webindex: 72485673
Company: Thomas Telford  Ltd
Description: Our client is long established building structures practice that has earned a first class reputation for quality and value. You will closely alongside and develop a thorough understanding of the business and its clients. The firm has a focus on London projects, with a weighting on the upper end of the private residential market. They also have considerable expertise within sustainable and low energy building design. You should be a technically experienced degree qualified building structures engineer who is familiar with British design codes and the residential sector. You will need some appreciation of commercial issues and have good client facing and communication skills. This is a successful small business and their busy workload means you will in turn be involved in client consultation, scheme design, production and delivery. Career prospects are excellent and there is an IStructE mentoring scheme for those who are aiming towards chartership.
