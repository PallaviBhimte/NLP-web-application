Description: The National IT Partnership is a training partnership specialising in the delivery of IT apprenticeships across the country. NiTP aim to provide the next generation of high quality IT Support Technicians to feed the demand for qualified IT professionals through the delivery of high quality Level 3 training including the CompTIA A and Microsoft professional qualifications. Working in partnership with Further Education colleges NiTP has become the provider of choice for many of the UK leading businesses. We are now actively seeking a professional to work as a supply IT Technical Trainer at our Academies. We are looking for professional IT Trainers, ideally with a working background in the IT sector, to deliver the training elements of our apprenticeship to designated groups. We are seeking candidates based in a central location with the flexibility to travel as and when required to our Academies in Birmingham, Tamworth, Walsall, Nottingham, Leicester and Liverpool. You will be required to mentor and guide our learners through the programme, working towards achieving set KPIs. We are looking for trainers who hold the CompTIA A, MCDST and MCITP. MCSE and MCSA would be an advantage as would experience of working on funded training programmes/apprenticeships. You will also hold a PTTLS or teaching qualification. You will be working closely with the apprentices enrolled with NiTP, and as such you must be able to relate well to the **** age groups. We are seeking trainers with a passion for IT and a passion for working with young people. The nature of this role requires a car owner/driver. There is also a requirement to pass an Enhanced CRB check.
