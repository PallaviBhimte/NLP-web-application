Description: If you are someone who wants to make a difference to peoples lives, helping them to live independently in their own homes by providing the highest quality care, contact us now. Home Instead Senior Care are looking for compassionate, caring and reliable people to provide companionship and support for older people in Harrogate and its surrounding areas. We have part time evening work available for the right person. The ability to show empathy and understanding are more important to us than formal experience and we welcome applicants from all ages . In return we offer flexible hours, excellent rates of pay, holiday pay, mileage, full training and ongoing personal development. You need to have your own transport and possess a full drivers license. Successful applicants will need to complete and pay for an enhanced Disclosure Barring Service check.
