Title: Information / Data Analyst
Webindex: 71679126
Company: Jobs at Capita
Description: An exciting opportunity for a motivated proactive individual with experience in data analysis / manipulation evaluation to join this BBC division, part of Capita Plc. The primary responsibility of this Role is to review data captured as a result of any Capita contact campaign and produce appropriate analysis / reporting for presentation to the Business, Client and 3rd Parties within agreed time scales. Using this analysis to provide suggested improvements / alternative campaigning that will increase revenue contribute to the success of Capita campaigns. KEY RESPONSIBILITIES  Design and implement reporting requirements for Campaigns as required.  Ensure all work is documented so that cover can be arranged in the event of sickness / holidays etc.  Produce presentations / reporting with analysis / trends patterns with graphics.  Embed Quality checking of all reports produced and the development of quality control processes.  Actively investigate and monitor new data sources and reporting methods as they become available.  Assist in the preparation of Month End reporting. The Campaign Information Analyst should have good data handling skills (ideally SQL skills), plus good financial acumen to assess cost benefits to the business some financial analysis. Advanced Excel including Visual Basic is required plus strong communication / presentation skills. Excellent long term opportunities.
