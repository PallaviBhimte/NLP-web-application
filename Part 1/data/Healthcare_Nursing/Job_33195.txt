Title: Skilled Senior Care Assistant
Webindex: 68450122
Company: St.marys International Education & Employment Ltd
Description: To have worked in a supervisory role for two years or more. To manage supervisory responsibilities as a part of their regular day to day duties . To have responsibility for front line supervision and supervise the careworkers and care assistants. To be in charge of the shift of the workers and take the responsibility for the smooth running of the service whilst they are on duty. To take care of the residents with regards to their physical, emotional and intellectual needs. To ensure all team members work to defined standard in the delivery of care. To liase with the manager and senior nurses to ensure effective communication for the benefit of clients and their families .
