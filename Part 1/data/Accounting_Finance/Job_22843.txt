Title: Customer Service Associates  Collections
Webindex: 65055810
Company: RBS
Description: Collections Officers are responsible for dealing with customers who are experiencing difficulties in making their repayments to the business. You ll be dealing with people from all walks of life, each with their own unique circumstances and this role is a careful balancing act that involves meeting their needs whilst looking after our best interests. You ll be speaking customers over the telephone to find solutions to their financial difficulties therefore you ll need to bring excellent communication and negotiation skills which you can use in a tactful and diplomatic way to build relationships and empathy with our customers. If you have previous experience of collections, that s great. If you haven t been in a collections role before but have the drive and enthusiasm to work in this challenging environment, you will be provided with all the training you need to succeed. We offer an excellent salary and benefits package which can be tailored to suit your individual needs. In addition to financial benefits, we offer a wide selection of exclusive lifestyle offers, development and learning programmes, services and support designed to help you manage and balance your own worklife priorities.
