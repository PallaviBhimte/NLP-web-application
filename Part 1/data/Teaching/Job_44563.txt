Title: L&D Officer
Webindex: 67389192
Description: Are you an experienced L D Officer? The job holder will contribute to the achievement of the company s strategic vision and aims for its employees, by conducting the full scope of activities required to deliver new employee orientation and ongoing training and evaluation. Take responsibility for, and proactively drive the design, development and effective delivery of induction training to employees within defined timescales by; Coordinating the delivery of the induction training programme to ensure that all new recruits undergo a level of training appropriate to their role in conjunction with the corporate induction. Managing the planning and rollout of induction programmes, across all departments Ensuring all class objectives are achieved; liaising with managers to ensure learning curve performance metrics are achieved according to predefined plan.
