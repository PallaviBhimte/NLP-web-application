Description: A Storage / Virtualisation Administrator is required to join the Global Leader in Talent Measurement, deploying solutions in over 150 countries and being present in over 50 countries they aid companies in selecting high quality candidates to meet their recruitment needs. The Storage / Virtualization Administrator will support and design the VMware ESX infrastructure and Storage systems across the organization to ensure high levels of data quality, availability, and security. This is an excellent opening for an experienced Storage / Virtualization Administrator to join an organisation that offers fantastic training and career prospects. The successful Storage / Virtualisation Administrator responsibilities will include but not be limited to working with system and database administrators to implement storage architectures that utilize industry best practices including Information Lifecycle Management, and Hierarchical Storage Management. You will implement redundant systems, policies, and procedures for disaster recovery and archiving to ensure effective protection and integrity of storage appliances and stored data assets. You will also be required to follow change control, incident response, and testing processes for modifications to storage appliances and software. Part of the role requires you to anticipate, mitigate, identify, respond to, and resolve issues with storage devices, storage access, and data retrieval. The Successful Storage / Virtualisation Administrator will have working technical experience with designing, building, installing, configuring, and supporting storage servers and management software, including HP SIM, MS SCOM. You will also have solid skills in Microsoft Server operating systems and handson Fiber Channel, iSCSI, SAN/NAS/DAS troubleshooting experience. To be knowledgeable of change management systems and applicable data retention practises and laws with the experience developing and submitting storage audits and compliance reports. Ideally you will have CCNA, MCITP, VMWare and ITIL foundation. Preference will be given to candidates who have project management experience.
