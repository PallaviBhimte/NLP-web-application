Title: Management Information Systems Assistant
Webindex: 71874857
Company: WESTON COLLEGE
Description: JOB PURPOSE Under the general direction of the Registry Manager to provide assistance with the collection and processing of data for College Management Information Systems. DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES Input the completed documents and to check the output records for accuracy. To access information within the database in response to enquiries concerning courses and students. To assist in the operation of other College Information systems. To comply with all requirements of the Data Protection Act with relation to College systems. To carry out such other duties as are required and as are commensurate with the grade of the post. Weston College is committed to safeguarding and promoting the welfare of children, young people and vulnerable adults and expects all staff and volunteers to share this commitment. Successful applicants will be required to complete an enhanced CRB check.
