Title: Oversight and Compliance Manager  Global Custody Operations
Webindex: 67158780
Company: Citifocus Limited
Description: City based subsidiary of Investment Bank wish to recruit an individual to manage and oversee all aspects of compliance and regulation control across their global custody operations. Applicants are therefore invited from individuals who offer several years experience specifically of Global Custody and/or ACD compliance procedures as well as the ability to interact well at all levels.
