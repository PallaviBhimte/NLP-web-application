Title: Qualified Dispensing Optician
Webindex: 68662938
Company: Healthline Optical Recruitment
Description: The Company This is a small, characterful independent with a strong belief in the power of providing a personalised patient experience. The practice has a rich history and a large, loyal, mostly private patient base. The elements of quality, traditional service sit alongside staff with personality and cuttingedge, middle to highend designer product. The Role  Being a highly visible personality within the store.  Advising and dealing with a range of patients during all parts of their experience; from the first conversation, to advising them on lens type, frames and styling, to aftersales care.  All aspects of dispensing.  Working smartly, flexibly and appropriately to maximise profitable sales.  Interacting and working confidently with the Owner and Optometrist and other members of staff.  Dependent on experience and ability, greater involvement in the management of the practice. The Person  You will be a confident, knowledgeable, characterful Dispensing Optician who is GOC registered.  You will have excellent interpersonal and communication skills, which will help you sell and advise effectively and appropriately.  You will be a good team player, but also someone with the ability to think and work independently.  You will be a multitasker who is organised and efficient.  You may have management experience or ability and a desire to be more involved in the wider management of the practice. Applicants of all ages are encouraged and appointment will be made on merit alone.
