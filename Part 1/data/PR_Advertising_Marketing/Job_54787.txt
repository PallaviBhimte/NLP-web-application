Description: Marketing Manager  Milton Keynes  **** inclusive of bonus. The Ashdown Group have been engaged by a national market leader to assist them in their search for a Marketing Manager to join their successful team based in Milton Keynes. This award winning company is looking for a marketing specialist to come on board to create and implement multiple marketing strategies. A large percentage of the role will involve traditional offline marketing, where you will be responsible for commissioning, creating and implementing high quality offline marketing communications that ultimately add value to the strategy of the business and deliver measurable results. To be considered for this role, you must be a superior writer, communicator and editor, with a good understanding of, and an interest in market research techniques and their use. In addition to this, you will be numerate and able to perform business analysis (forecasting, break even analysis, internal rates of return), whilst also be intelligent in presentations of statistics, therefore possess excellent analytical skills. Furthermore, you must understand best practice in media, digital, offline and creative planning, be digitally literate and capable of creating and delivering social/ digital media strategies. You must also be a capable handler of PR and weekly digital communication and experienced in creating newsletters. You will understand and cope well within a highvisibility, highpressure environment during times of dynamic change. With this in mind, the suitable candidate will have marketing processing procurement experience from a large corporate background. Ideally you will come from a blue chip professional services environment, and knowledge of the property sector is highly desirable. You will be responsible for the delivery and creation of marketing collateral, and as such will require excellent writing, editing, and proof reading skills, with an expert knowledge in MS Word, Excel and PowerPoint. The ideal candidate will be tenacious, flexible, driven and passionate about effective communication, who thrives in high pressure, unsupervised environments. You will show solid commercial judgement and awareness of the need for profit. It is expected the candidate will be educated to degree level, or the equivalent with professional qualifications. In return, you will receive a competitive salary plus bonus and the opportunity to work with an awardwinning market leader who invest heavily in their staff and are keen to promote from within.
