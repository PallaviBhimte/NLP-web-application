Title: Technical Superintendent  Offshore Vessels

Company: Advanced Resource Managers Ltd
Description: A well established Offshore vessel owner operator in Aberdeen, seeks a Technical Superintendent to start immediately. Reporting to the fleet manager, you will be responsible for an assigned fleet of Emergency response vessels. The position will involve regular Budgeting, inspections, risk assessments, including planned maintenance and dry dockings Due to the nature of the vessel type and operations, regular monitoring and management is critical  to ensure vessels are available to respond immediately to any incident or emergency offshore, you must be an excellent communicator and attention to detail. To qualify for this role you must hold a Chief engineer class 1 COC, and sailed to either Chief engineer or 1st Engineer on similar offshore vessel types  shore based experience is desirable but not essential.
