Title: Senior Expense Management Accountant
Webindex: 68177553
Company: Hays Senior Finance
Description: This is a commercial finance opportunity in a leading international business which has grown extensively through a combination of acquisitions and organic growth. From their London office they manage their international business which spans a number of operating divisions across the UK, Europe, Asia Pacific and South America. In this position, you will be responsible for key financial decisions across corporate support functions which include HR, Marketing and IT. Your success will be achieved by undertaking detailed analysis of management accounts and helping the Directors understand what the drivers are to business performance. To help the company proactively manage costs and expense you will also drive improved forecasting and budgeting across these functions. You will be joining a high performing and driven finance team who are gaining unrivalled experience as a result of the companies ambitious growth and acquisition plans. This is an opportunity that can really progress your career to the next level. We are therefore looking for fully qualified accountant (ACCA, ACA, CIMA) who wants to work closely with the business. You will need to be working in an Insurance or Financial Services business and be able to show recent experience of expense and cost management. Because of the business partnering elements of this role, you will need to be confident and have the ability to explain financial reports to non finance staff across the company.
