Title: Adult Education Teachers
Webindex: 69145795
Company: North Yorkshire County Council
Description: The Adult Learning and Skills Service are seeking individuals to join their workforce of parttime hourly paid sessional teachers to deliver planned courses throughout the year. The service offers an extensive range of courses at all levels including vocational and nonaccredited courses. The courses are based in community venues around the county. You will be required to hold a teaching qualification, have experience of teaching adults, possess good academic qualifications in your subject specialism and demonstrate evidence of continuous professional development. A flexible approach to programme delivery, which could include evenings is required. In the Craven area we are seeking teachers for the delivery of courses to support trade union members in Beginners IT, Health Safety at work (trade unions), domestic violence, women in the workplace, employment rights, representing workers rights. Also, we are seeking teachers for the delivery of Early Years courses.
