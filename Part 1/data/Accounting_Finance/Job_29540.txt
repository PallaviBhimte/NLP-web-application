Title: Fiduciary Technician
Webindex: 72338144
Company: Capita Resourcing
Description: An exceptional opportunity for an enthusiastic, target driven individual to join a fastpaced, growing team environment. As a Fiduciary Technician you will enjoy variety and career development opportunities that are second to none. This position would suit someone with strong customer service skills and some accounts experience who would like to utilise their skills within the insurance sector.  To provide a professional accounting service in respect of business transacted on behalf of clients. Undertake credit control activities, reconcile settlements, raise payment requests and resolve queries in line with timeframes set within the function.  Contribute to the achievement of service level agreements, key performance indicators and business objectives.  Build and maintain strong working relationships, including effective communication, with colleagues, clients, insurers and third parties.  Undertake research and investigation to resolve issues and achieve the best possible outcome for its clients, including clearance of outstanding balances.  Act upon payment conditions and deadlines as appropriate.  Contribute to internal and external meetings and projects as required.  Participate in internal and external audits as appropriate. Excellent communication skills are essential, good Excel skills & a miniumum of GCSE Grade C, or above, in Maths & English.
