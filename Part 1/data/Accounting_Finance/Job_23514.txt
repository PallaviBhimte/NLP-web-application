Title: Consultant Relationship Support
Webindex: 67102990
Company: Argyll Scott
Description: Overview of the Role The successful candidate will already have an understanding of the investment consulting model as it relates to institutional clients and investment managers. They will not, however, necessarily have managed consultant relationships directly before. Main Duties Supporting the two London based consultant relationship managers, allowing them to spend as much time as possible engaging directly with consultants. Dealing with adhoc consultant enquiries, talking to the relevant consultant to ensure a full understanding of their requirements. Attending premeets for research meetings and followingup on any actions, working with the fund managers as necessary. Working with the RFP both within the business and externally. An ability to articulate investment processes, and understand what differentiates them. Commercial awareness.
