Title: Bus Escort Attendant
Webindex: 70314895
Description: ASA Recruitment are looking to recruit relief Bus Escort Attendants to work in Glasgow. You will be required to assist with the transport of service users with learning disabilities to and from Day Care facilities ensuring the safety of passengers and providing physical assistance whilst boarding or alighting the vehicle which may include the use of mechanical aids. Previous experience in a similar role is desirable but care experience working with personal care is essential. Shifts are between 8am to 10am and 3pm to 5pm Monday to Friday.
