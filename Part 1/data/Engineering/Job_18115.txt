Title: Technologist Software Safety (Guided Weapons)
Webindex: 69249308
Company: Matchtech Group plc.
Description: Introduction Software Safety Engineer required to act as the independent signatory for safety related software, and for Product Safety (Guided Weapon Systems). Important Developed and implemented safety related software standards. The Job Role Purpose: Independent Signatory for Safety Related Software, and for Product Safety Accountabilities: The main role of Technologist Software Safety (TSS) is to independently examine, review and approve the design of software solutions that are considered to be a constituent part of a SafetyRelated function. The TSS is also expected to operate as a Guided Weapon Safety Officer (GWSO). As such, the TSS is in a unique position within the company to provide safety assurance to Product Development Project Teams, Senior Management and the Customer(s), that the design, implementation and testing of such software is compatible with safety standards for such software amp; products. Responsible to the Head of Product Safety (UK). as Technologist Software Safety:  For reviewing and approving plans, specifications, analysis reports, procedures, instructions and documents containing descriptions and information about SafetyRelated software products  To have a good understanding of SafetyRelated software standards and their implementation  For performing SafetyRelated software audit activities when required by contract or in support of an Independent Safety Auditor As Guided Weapon Safety Officer:  To review and approve plans, specifications, analysis reports, procedures, instructions and documents containing descriptions and information about SafetyRelated products  To attend, internal and external project/product Safety Panel meetings and Customer Safety Committee meetings.  To review and approve, when satisfied, Certificates of Design To apply for this position, candidates must be eligible to live and work in the UK
