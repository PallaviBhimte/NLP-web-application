Title: Apprentice Front of House/Kitchen Assistant
Webindex: 69551459
Company: NS Care Ltd
Description: This role is looking for an apprentice front of house/Kitchen assistant. Your duties will be food preparation, serving drinks, meeting and greeting customers, general housekeeping of tea rooms, liasing with kitchen staff, taking orders, serving food, multitasking where needed and team work. This apprenticeship you will recieve an NVQ level 2 in Hospitality Services and a certificate in Catering Principles.
