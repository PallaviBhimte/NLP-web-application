Title: Finance Modelling Analyst
Webindex: 72581019
Company: Toner Graham
Description: This prestigious international organisation requires a high calibre Analyst to join one of its business units. The purpose of the role will be to drive improved performance by analysing KPIs and providing good quality financial modelling expertise to the wider business. This will extend to identifying opportunities for hitting KPIs and challenging them where appropriate through the use of Excel. You will also support the business in the commercial analysis of major projects and product launches by developing a variety of models to evaluate their viability, in the process liaising with a wide variety of internal stakeholders. To be considered for this opportunity you will need to be a qualified accountant (ACA, ACCA, CIMA) with outstanding communication skills and experience of a similar analytical position in a complex, fastmoving organisation.
