Title: Higher Education Quality Coordinator
Webindex: 71866610
Company: WESTON COLLEGE
Description: Following the very successful IQER and gaining directly funded HE numbers the College is looking to appoint a Quality Coordinator responsible for the assurance and improvements required in HE to meet the new quality framework. The successful candidate will be an outstanding, dynamic and innovative individual and will join the Quality and Learning Team. This individual will be responsible leading the coordination and monitoring of the colleges Higher Education provision. You will need to be fully conversant with the challenges and opportunity of Review of Colleges Higher Education and the UK Quality Code. As part of the team they will ensure quality systems are implemented and that improvement strategies and interventions are both timely and effective. Weston College is committed to safeguarding and promoting the welfare of children, young people and vulnerable adults and expects all staff and volunteers to share this commitment. Successful applicants will be required to complete an enhanced CRB check.
