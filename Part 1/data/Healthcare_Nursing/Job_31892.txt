Title: HOME SUPPORT ASSISTANTS
Webindex: 66750270
Company: At Home with...Helen McArdle Care
Description: We are looking for experienced home care workers to join our expanding service which offers bespoke care packages to older people in Ponteland, Gosforth and Jesmond areas. You must be able to drive and have transport and mileage is paid. You must be able to work in a person centred way directly with service users, communicate effectively and be able to work off your own initiative. Main duties include personal care, help with household management tasks, enabling and escorting services. A care qualification is an advantage but full training will be given. This is an excellent opportunity for home carers who are dedicated to delivering high quality care.
