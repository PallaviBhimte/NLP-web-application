Title: Shift Leader/Senior Support Worker
Webindex: 68067671
Company: Hays
Description: Our client, a well established care provider based in Warminster is looking for a Shift Leader. You will be working within a residential setting as part of a team supervising and providing holistic approach to young people with emotional and behavioural problems. Shift Leader will be working with a Support Worker acting as the senior member of staff and will hold key responsibility for young people in care. You will implement care plans and carry out risk assessments, ensure the work is carried out within the Health and Safety guidelines, develop good working relationships with the young people, their families and external agencies. Shift Leader will be responsible for maintaining accurate records, update care plans and follow Every Child Matters guidelines when working with young people. All applicants need to have an NVQ level 3 in Children and Young People and previous experience of working in a residential setting. In return the company offers excellent training facilities and career development.
