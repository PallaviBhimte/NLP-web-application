Description: Sheridan Maine Accountancy and Finance is recruiting an Operational Finance Manager for the headquarters of a high profile manufacturing business in Poole. You will be a robust, confident individual at ease working with all levels in the business with persistence, drive and enthusiasm to get the job done. Also required: strong IT skills particularly SAP, Excel and Word combined with deep analytical skills and a systematic and methodical approach to problem solving and data analysis, experience of a manufacturing environment and best practice manufacturing principles and an excellent understanding of manufacturing finance systems and controls.
