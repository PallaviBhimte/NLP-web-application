Title: Capex Analyst

Description: My client is an international market leader in their field and have a large operation based in Peterborough. Owing to a desire to continually grow their organisation, and also grow their Peterborough based finance team, a role has arisen within the reporting team as a Capital Expenditure analyst. In order to be considered for this role YOU MUST be qualified to a minimum level of AAT and looking to study further, and also have a high level of MS Excel skills and strong be a strong communicator. In addition to this the candidates who are currently studying toward an ACCA qualification are preferred as full study support is provided as part of the benefits package.
