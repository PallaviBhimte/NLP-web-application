Title: New Year, New You, New Career Become a Care Worker
Webindex: 66601469
Description: Fancy doing a job that is rewarding and actually makes a difference in people's lives? Becoming a Care Worker means that you will help people to remain independent in their own home. You will become an integral part of their day to day routine and you will become an appreciated part of their daily lives. You will help the service user to meet their personal needs in a sensitive manner and to a high standard, providing dignity and independence at all times. Flexible working hours are available, we are looking for both full and part time workers. We are also looking for evening and weekend staff. No experience is necessary as all training will be provided.
