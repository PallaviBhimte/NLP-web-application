Description: IT Security Officer to work for a household known Public Services organisation. Our customer has national coverage and thousands of staff across the UK. Due to growth within their IT Department they are now seeking an IT Security Officer to join their team based in Cardiff. The nature of the role will be to act will as the regional focus for information security issues by ensuring all systems and processes are compliant with national security guidelines, current legislation, policy and best practice. This is carried out through monitoring, inspection and risk assessment of individual properties. Additionally, you will ensure the organisation compliance with the ACPO/ACPOS Information Systems Community Security (CSP) and assisting the Information Security Officer to implement the Information Security Policy (physical, procedural, personnel and technical security), System Security Policies and Security Operating Procedures. The ideal candidate will have experience of Information Security within a large organisation. This will include monitoring of processes and policies. ISEB or a equivalent IT Security Certification is seen as highly desirable.
