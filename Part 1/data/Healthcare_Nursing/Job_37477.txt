Description: Our client, a diverse pharmaceutical business a major supplier of healthcare products to the Retail Pharmacy, Hospital FMCG sectors has an opportunity for a Senior Analyst I or II to join the Technical Development Dept. The successful candidate will carry out a varied range of duties, including the establishment of analytical methods within the company and transferring analytical methods to other third parties. Additional duties will include the development and validation of analytical methods to current regulatory standards and performing ICH stability tests on products and providing analytical support to other functions on site. Applicants should be educated to at least BSc in Chemistry and you should have extensive experience in bioactive pharmaceutical analysis, in particular HPLC/GC (High Performance Liquid Chromatology / Gas Chromatography) and also possess a good understanding of the regulatory requirements for analytical method validation. Experience in intercompany method transfers would also be desirable. In addition to analytical skills, the position also requires the ability to write comprehensive technical reports to a high standard. The successful candidate should be able to work on their own initiative and contribute to and work as part of the team. With thriving Export Contract Manufacturing Divisions in place, this is an exciting time for someone to join this rapidly growing organisation. The organisation is also benefiting from significant financial investment. In return, on offer is a competitive market salary plus bonus and benefits, as well as the opportunity to work with an industry leading organisation who are looking to attract the very best talent in the marketplace.
