Description: Domiciliary Care Training Officer  North London My clients, a wellestablished and reputable care organisation providing highquality domiciliary care in the community to vulnerable adults in their own homes throughout north London, are looking for a knowledgeable, organised, professional and qualityfocused candidate to work as Training Officer. You will be running training course and events, and ensuring that any new legislation is implemented into this training. Applicants from a nonlearning domiciliary care training background are of course more than welcome to apply.
