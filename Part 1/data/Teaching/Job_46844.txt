Description: Part time **** to **** Required from 15th April 2013, the Governors of St. Joseph’s RC Primary School, Blackhall and Our Lady of Lourdes RC Primary School wish to appoint a School Chaplain, on a 1 year temporary contract. 1.5 days per week, on a flexible basis, term time only The Chaplain will have a central role in implementing the school mission statements and work with the Head Teachers and both whole school teams in leading and developing the Catholic life of the schools. Through their work and witness, they will contribute to the spiritual and prayer life of the school communities. It is essential that the successful candidate is a committed and practising Catholic with a good understanding and knowledge of Catholic Faith. Excellent interpersonal, communication and organizational skills along with an understanding are also required. Hours – 8.30 am  4.00 pm, term time only, days to be worked on a flexible basis.
