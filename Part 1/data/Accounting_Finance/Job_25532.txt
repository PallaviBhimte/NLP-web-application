Description: Management Accountant (CCAB, CIMA, Financial) BLUF**** Blackpool ****  **** per annum Fixed term contract for one year initially Our client aims to ensure outstanding learning opportunities for all students on the Fylde Coast by facilitating the highest standards of teaching, learning and assessment in each phase of learning. As part of this our client is setting up a multiacademy trust, which will seek to sponsor local academy schools. This role will be an integral part of the establishment of the multiacademy trust and the links with the sponsored academies. As Management Accountant, you will support the Board of Directors and will report on a day to day basis to the Director of Finance and Personnel. Your main responsibilities will include the initial set up of procedures relating to the multi academy trust such as the accurate and efficient preparation of financial and management reports for the Board on a timely basis. You will also be responsible for liaising with the Heads of Finance and Business Support in the sponsored academies ensuring financial administration and reporting procedures are in line with the multi academy trust vision. This will also include the set up and supervision of third party services being provided to the sponsored academies by either of the members of the school. In order to be successful in this role, it is essential that candidates have GCSE qualifications (or equivalent) in Maths and English at Grade AC, will ideally hold a recognised CCAB accountancy qualification/part qualification and, in the absence of an accountancy qualification, they must hold a degree level qualification (or equivalent). With experience of production of management reports and financial accounts, the successful Management Accountant will also have experience of Charity Commission and Companies House’ returns and requirements. It is also essential that candidates have good general IT skills including experience of using Finance Systems’ report writers and, whilst not essential, experience of accounting within an educational environment would be advantageous. The role requires someone who can work on their own initiative, is confident in dealing with people at a senior level, proficient in the use of Excel and with an excellent eye for detail.
