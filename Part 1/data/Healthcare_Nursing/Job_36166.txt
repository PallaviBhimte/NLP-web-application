Job Profile As a junior SLT you will be working directly with a defined specialist caseload of students with complex needs associated with their diagnosis to assess their communication needs and provide input into their waking day curriculum, both in classrooms and residential settings. As part of a full multidisciplinary team onsite, you will also work with all staff across the school to support the implementation of the therapeutic approach. You will also be part of a company wide SLT service and contribute to the service enhancement through intervention development, meetings and conferences. Full induction training is given. You will also learn from some of the most dedicated and inspirational people in our profession and just like our young people and Service Users you will be learning all the time through our ongoing mentoring and support. Candidate Profile To be successful in this role you will be excited by the Cambian vision and values: passion, knowledge and care and will relish an opportunity to make a difference. You will have had undergraduate experience of working with client group and in the school setting. You will have some knowledge of strategies and approaches appropriate to the client group an have an understanding of the nature of challenging behaviour. Successful candidates will have to meet the person specification in order to be offered this post.
