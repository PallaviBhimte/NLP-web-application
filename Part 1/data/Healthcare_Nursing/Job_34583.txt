Title: NHS Accounting Costings
Webindex: 69260664
Description: An exciting opportunity for a partqualified accountant to use their costings experience in an NHS role. This temporary role will involve working with the finance teams and clinicians in understanding the true cost of treatment on a patient by patient basis. Within the NHS this is known as Activity Based Costings or Patient Level Costings. The successful candidate will have advanced Excel skills and strong analytical skills in reviewing ongoing costs. The purpose of the role will be to identify accurate income and costs related to activity therefore any candidates with costings experience and wish to use their skills in a special project not just linked to finance would find this role an interesting challenge. We are keen to hear from individuals who may have experience of a costings management system known as Prodacapo. If you can demonstrate the skills required, please submit your CV online.
