Description: A high  end, niche manufacturing organisation is seeking a site controller based on its plant in Staffordshire. In order to apply for this position, you must be a fully qualified accountant and be currently working within or have recently worked within a manufacturing environment. You will be responsible for forecasting and business planning, implementing cost reduction processes and implementing KPIs and overhead controls. You will be a hands on individual with good communications skills who is passionate about the manufacturing sector.
