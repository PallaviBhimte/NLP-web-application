Title: Clinical Director
Webindex: 69897246
Company: YMCA of Greater Cleveland
Description: GENERAL DESCRIPTION Under the general direction of the Executive Director, the Clinical Director is accountable for the management of the clinical staff to ensure the quality of counseling services for homeless and chemically dependent men. QUALIFICATIONS Master s Degree in social work, counseling or related field from an accredited college or university required. Candidate must possess independent licensure in clinical social work or counseling (LISW, PCC or LICDC). Must have a minimum of five years of experience in the field of social work, counseling or chemical dependency with a minimum of two years supervisory experience. Working with certifying agencies such as ODADAS, CARF, Medicaid or the Veterans Administration preferred.
