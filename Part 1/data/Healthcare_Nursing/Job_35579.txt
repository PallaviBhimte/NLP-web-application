Description: Our client are a national organisation with an excellent reputation, for providing high quality care to adults with learning disabilities and mental health problems. As a successful candidate, you will have experience of supporting adults with learning disabilities and mental health problems . You will be passionate about helping individuals to partake in activities and community events and help them to develop confidence, skills and independence.
