Title: Assistant Front of House Supervisor
Webindex: 67441441
Company: Seven Stars Inn
Description: Job Purpose: To control the front of house operation in the absence of the supervisor. Providing a pleasant dining experience by maintaining ambiance; helping out with table preparation and food delivery; building and maintaining customer rapport; recommending menus; supervising staff. Please note you will be required to stay on the premises up to three nights a week as part of this role. Duties:  Maintains safe, secure, and clean environment by establishing, following, and enforcing sanitation standards and procedures; complying with legal regulations.  Maintains cleanliness of restaurant and bar area according to cleaning schedule. Is proactive in ensuring front of house presentation is kept to a high standard  Meets restaurant operational standards by implementing productivity, quality, and customerservice standards; resolving problems; recommending system improvements.  Responsible for setting up the restaurant to the required standards, and for closing down and cashing up at the end of the night.  Assists hotel residents during the checkin process and for the remainder of their stay when required.  Maintains ambiance by controlling lighting, background music, linen service, glassware, dinnerware, and utensil quality and placement; monitoring food presentation and service.  Maintains customer service by helping wait staff with table preparation and food delivery.  Builds customer rapport by establishing relationships with regular customers; greeting customers; interceding in misunderstandings and differences between waiting staff and customers; resolving complaints.  Controls costs by minimising waste.  Accomplishes restaurant goals by accepting ownership for accomplishing new and different requests; exploring opportunities to add value to job accomplishments.
