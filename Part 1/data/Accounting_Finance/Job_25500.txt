Title: Purchase Ledger Coordinator
Webindex: 68702773
Company: Adecco Group
Description: Duties will include but are not limited to: Portray a positive image of the company and its values in a professional manner at all times. Accurate and timely processing of all invoices. Ensuring that all invoices have appropriate backup documentation. Liaise with authorised signatories to obtain approval for all invoices in a timely fashion. Liaising with suppliers to maintain accurate records, including change of address / bank details. Maintaining an accurate filing system.
