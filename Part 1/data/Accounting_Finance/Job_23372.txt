Title: Part Qualified Bookkeeper
Webindex: 67100170
Company: SF Group
Description: The role is a cross functional role where you are required to provide accurate financial information and liaise and report with non finance personnel. You will take ownership of the preparation of annual budgets, the maintenance of the nominal ledger, and bank reconciliations. The successful candidate must be comfortable working in a fast paced environment with a desire to lead improvement, innovation and change. You will be either studying or newly qualified CIMA, ACCA with advanced Excel Skills. The individual will be very commercial minded and previous experience working in a manufacturing environment would be advantageous. This is an exciting opportunity to join a growing manufacturing business based in Grantham. The role will be a stand alone position within the accounts department and is an excellent opportunity to make the position your own. You will benefit from an excellent starting salary and benefits package.Only applicants with a suitable amount of UK based experience and who have a legal right to work within the UK will be considered.
