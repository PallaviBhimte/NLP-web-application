Title: APPRENTICE NURSERY ASSISTANT
Webindex: 71341647
Description: Term Time Only. This is an Apprenticeship. The employer has given an assurance the apprenticeship is government funded. An Apprentice is required to provide day to day care to all children following daily routines taking part in topic lead activities giving support & guidance to all children. Help & support children with toileting & snack times. Take part in outings, outside activities & planning for weekly routines with senior team members. Work alongside other preschool practitioners on a daily basis. Follow all safeguarding & Equality & Diversity policies & procedures. You will be expected to complete an Apprenticeship Intermediate Level in Children & Young People's Workforce and Level 1 Functional Skills in English, Maths & IT. You must be caring & supportive to all children, keen to learn. Enhanced CRB Check required employer meet cost.
