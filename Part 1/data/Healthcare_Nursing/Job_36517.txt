Description: Applications are invited for this highly successful research partnership. This fixedterm (2 years), position is ideally suited to doctors training in gastrointestinal surgery who have completed BSTlevel training. MRCS is preferred but not essential. The position is funded by Spire Manchester Hospital with the appointee carrying out RMO duties at this busy hospital. It is desirable that the successful applicant holds both APLS & PALS. The core role for the appointee is to provide high quality resident medical cover for a busy surgical unit. The weekly timetable facilitates excellent opportunities for research. The expected outline of this project is to evaluate the new technique of arteryfirst pancreaticoduodenectomy. It is expected that the appointee will submit his/her work for the degree of MD. An honorary clinical contract will be provided and the appointee will have the opportunity to participate in the clinical programme of the Hepatobiliary Unit at the MRI. Terms and conditions of employment are set by Spire Manchester Hospital. The post will be supervised by Professor Siriwardena. Enquiries regarding the post should be addressed to Dawn Davies, Matron, Spire Manchester Hospital.
