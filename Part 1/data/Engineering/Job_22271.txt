Title: Safety Advisor
Webindex: 72479693
Company: N.R.L Ltd 
Description: Our client, an international engineering firm, is looking for an experienced Safety Officer to oversee the completion of a major construction project. The ideal candidate would be in possession of a NEBOSH General or NEBOSH Construction Certificate. Current Security Clearance is preferred due to immediate start date. Duties will include: Offering advice on following safety procedures in addition to reviewing and improving the existing safety structure. Investigating incidents and conducting audits of daytoday work on plant to ensure safety standards are being met. The successful candidate would also be responsible for conducting safety briefs, arranging and delivering safety training, ToolBox Talks and HSC meetings. Previous heavy construction / power station experience would be preferred.
