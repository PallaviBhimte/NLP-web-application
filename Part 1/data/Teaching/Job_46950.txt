Title: Schools Liaison and Events Officer
Webindex: 71337866
Company: RICHMOND UPON THAMES COLLEGE
Description: The job purpose is to effectively promote the College and its course offer to key stakeholders, particularly prospective students and parents, through a number of communication strategies including events, presentations, printed information. To strengthen and develop relationships with a number of schools and careers advisers located both in the borough of Richmond and outside the area. To work with key colleagues on planning and promoting the College and developing creative, practical and effective schools liaison strategies. To project manage specific tasks as directed by the Departmental Manager, Marketing Communications or other senior college personnel.
