Title: Plastic Fabricator
Webindex: 69142479
Description: Job Purpose: Fabricates components by analysing requirements. Duties:  Develops fabrication projects by analysing work orders, prints, and completed assembly; calculating requirements; studying plastics; determining appropriate joining techniques.  Collects equipment and materials by studying specifications and component requirements; ordering supplies.  Fabricates components by using equipment; settingup and measuring assemblies; selecting appropriate method.  Verifies fabrication results by inspecting and testing work.  Documents actions by completing records.  Maintains safe work environment by following procedures; complying with legal regulations.  Keeps equipment operating by following operating instructions; troubleshooting breakdowns; maintaining supplies; performing preventive maintenance; calling for repairs.  Updates job knowledge by participating in educational opportunities.  Accomplishes manufacturing and organisation mission by completing related results as needed. Skills/Qualifications: Plastic Fabrication, Supply Management, Documentation Skills, Basic Safety, Equipment Maintenance, Power Tools, Lifting, Analysing Information, Dependability, Independence, Customer Service
