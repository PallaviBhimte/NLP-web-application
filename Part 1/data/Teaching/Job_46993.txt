Title: Motor Vehicle Trainer  Work Based Learning

Company: Spirit Resourcing
Description: A Training Provider with contractdelivery agreements throughout the UK seeks a Motor Vehicle Assessor and Tutor for their Motor Vehicle department based in Leeds. You will work closely with your colleague to manage a caseload of learners who are undertaking a range of courses and apprenticeships. The courses available cover maintenance, repair and operations from light through to heavy vehicles. The Centre is equipped with specialist workshops that will enable you to delivery practical training sessions that fully replicate working within the industry. You will deliver all of your training at the centre to small groups of learners. The Training Provider has excellent links with the MV sector and is able to place many learners into apprenticeships, which helps learners maintain a positive and committed attitude towards their education. The other aspect of your role as an Assessor will be on a onetoone basis with apprentice learners and you will visit them at their place of work. All of your Employers will be based in the region and you will have the opportunity to discuss your learners` progress with their employers and report on their progress to management. Therefore, good communication skills at a senior level are important. To interview for this role you must be an experienced Assessor/Tutor within the Motor Vehicle/Automotive sector and possess vocational experience of working within this specialist field. An A**** qualification or equivalent is essential. Benefits include mileage and full expenses, mobile and laptop and 25 days annual leave per annum plus pension.
