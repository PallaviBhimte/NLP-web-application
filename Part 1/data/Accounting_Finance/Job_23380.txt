Description: Working in over 100 countries worldwide, this global charitable organisation is looking to recruit a Global Audit Manager to support the Board of Trustees and review the governance and risk management effectiveness across all locations. With this, knowledge of major donors, such as DfID, USAID, EU, etc., is advantageous and experience of working in diverse workplaces will be important.
