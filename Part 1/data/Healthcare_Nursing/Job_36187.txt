Title: Adult Care Worker
Webindex: 70599095
Description: A compassionate and resourceful individuals are required to work as both part of a team and on a one to one basis with adults with learning disabilities. Arts and Crafts experience would be an advantage as our customers enjoy and are stimulated by creative activities. Duties will include meal preparation, personal care and medication prompts. You will also be required to escort customers to social activities. A full driving Licence is desirable but is not essential. Experience of care work is desirable but not essential as you will be given full training including a fully funded level 2 qualification. A pay enhancement will be paid upon successful completion of the level 2 qualification and progression opportunities are available. Applicants are required to provide an enhanced disclosure the expense of which will be met by the applicant.
