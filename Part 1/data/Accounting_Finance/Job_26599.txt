Title: Support Share Plan Administrator
Webindex: 69260353
Description: Our client based in the town centre of Ipswich is seeking an entry level Support Share Plan Administrator to deliver first class outsourced share plan administration to UK and International corporate clients to join their team. The job role is a varied one and will include updating and accurately maintaining client employee database records. A large part of the role will include preparation and organisation of accurate and timely mailings to the clients employees, dealing professionally with emails and telephone queries. You will also be responsible in assisting in the reconciliation of share and cash positions and the purchase and sale of shares for the clients and their employees. This position also involves administration duties including filing and scanning documents.
