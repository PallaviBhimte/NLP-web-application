Title: Technical Project Manager  Avaya
Webindex: 72127915
Company: Harvey Nash plc
Description: Technical Project Manager to provide technical project management for UK, panEMEA and global projects, with specific responsibility for advising clients on implementation and technical issues, and managing multiple concurrent and often complex single and multisite implementations. The role of the Project Manager is to plan, execute, and finalize projects according to strict deadlines and within budget. Candidates must have a good understanding of Project Management methodologies (Prince2, PMP or PMI) and project delivery capacity, good knowledge of Unified Communications and contact centre technologies, especially the Avaya Aura product set and a broad understanding of IP Telephony. Candidates must have experience of matrix management and a good understanding of voice and data networks and of managing the rollout of often complex technical UC and contact centre solutions across multiple sites.
