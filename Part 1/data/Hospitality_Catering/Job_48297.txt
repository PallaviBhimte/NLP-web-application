Title: Kitchen Staff
Webindex: 66537257
Company: J.D.Wetherspoons
Description: No previous experience is required although a friendly, polite attitude and good customer service skills is needed. Applicants need to be able to communicate well at all levels and be able to work unsupervised in a busy environment. Duties include ensuring that all food and associated products are prepared, cooked, served and presented in accordance with company standards, adhering to company stock control procedures and ensuring the kitchen is kept clean and hygienic at all times.
