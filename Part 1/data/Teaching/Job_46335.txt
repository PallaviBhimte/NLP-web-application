The UCL School of Life and Medical Sciences (SLMS) is one of the world’s largest aggregations of academics in biomedical, life and population health sciences and has an established track record of biomedical innovation, commercialisation and collaboration with industrial partners. The successful candidates will play a leading role in working with this biomedical community to increase UCL’s ability to develop new approaches to diagnosis and treatment of disease through developing and maintaining new partnerships with industry. The post holders will work closely with colleagues in Enterprise Operations, especially the Corporate Partnerships Managers with similar roles, as well as with the ViceDeans of Enterprise, the UCL Translational Research Office and UCL Business. Two posts are available. The successful candidates will have a highly entrepreneurial approach to identifying opportunities for new highvalue partnerships and bringing them to fruition, and the interpersonal skills to manage a diverse set of relationships. The appointees will have a level of knowledge generally acquired by postgraduate academic study (ideally in a subject relevant to biomedical research) and professional commercial experience. Effective delivery will depend on excellent abilities to communicate with leaders in academia, the National Health Service and commercial partners.
