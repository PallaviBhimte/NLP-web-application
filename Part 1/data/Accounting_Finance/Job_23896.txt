Title: Chief Financial Accountant
Webindex: 68013852
Company: Walker Hamill
Description: The successful candidate will be responsible for a staff of approximately twenty. You will need to lead and motivate the team, understand where resource gaps exist and where process and resource efficiencies can be created. Moreover, you will cultivate and promote a robust control culture across both the team & wider organisation. You will provide first class technical expertise to senior management and provide high level oversight for financial reporting. You will also manage the relationship with the external auditors and oversee the successful delivery of internal finance projects. The successful individual must possess a recognised accountancy qualification (ACA, ACCA or equivalent), strong financial reporting experience and significant banking or financial services sector experience. You will have successfully managed a team and ideally have exposure to product control and treasury. You must also have an excellent technical grasp of International Financial Reporting Standards.
