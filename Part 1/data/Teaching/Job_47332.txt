Title: Assessment & Training Coordinator  Electrical
Webindex: 71866611
Company: WESTON COLLEGE
Description: The successful candidate will manage all Electrical Work Based Learning programmes in line with Key Performance Indicators, as well as managing the on site assessment of Apprenticeships. You must be a qualified Assessor with the A**** Award (or equivalent) and have knowledge of the Further Education curriculum, especially in relation to work based qualification. Applicants must be highly motivated and committed to working within a team. Weston College is committed to safeguarding and promoting the welfare of children, young people and vulnerable adults and expects all staff and volunteers to share this commitment. Successful applicants will be required to complete an enhanced CRB check.
