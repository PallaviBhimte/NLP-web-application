Description: A permanent role has arisen within the Financial Reporting function of our market leading, branded client based in Swindon. You'll also be responsible for ensuring that the integrity of the Treasury financial information is accurate and correct while giving assurances to Senior Management on the reliability of the Treasury financial results. The role will require clear communication on the reported result whilst being able to explain the drivers for any changes. You will be responsible for providing clear management information to explain the control environment for Treasury financial results.
