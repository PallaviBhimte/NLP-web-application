Title: Auditor / Stock Counter
Webindex: 71756005
Company: RGIS
Description: THE ROLE  When performing an inventory, stock counters called auditors are required to count the inventory in the store, these auditors are supervised by a Team Leader. JOB REQUIREMENTS Pass the online Certification/Training Programme to perform the duties of a Team Leader. Supervise a team of inventory counters in large inventories in relation to major retailers. Physically count stock when required. Monitor the progress of the inventory and resolve any issues. Ability to perform repetitive work, perform under stress and make sound judgements. Ability to follow instructions from Store personnel and line manager. Must have own car and have full license. PERSON DESCRIPTION Strong communication organisational skills. Able to work independently or as part of a team. Excellent timekeeper reliable. Motivated with a cando attitude. Flexible.
