Title: Trainee Furnace Instruments Engineer
Webindex: 69759952
Company: Independent Forgings & Alloys
Description: Joining an experienced team we are looking for a committed person who can develop specialist skills and build a well rewarded career. This role would suit an apprentice trained maintenance engineer with electrical engineering or electronics experience or gas fired furnace experience and related qualifications. You should be PC literate and be able to keep records to the highest standards in line with the requirements from external auditors.
