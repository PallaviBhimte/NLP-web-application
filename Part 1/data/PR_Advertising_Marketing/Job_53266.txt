Title: Senior Internal Communications Manager
Webindex: 68379468
Description: My client an international Insurance organisation is seeking to recruit a Senior Communications Manager with a focus on Internal Communications. This is a fantastic opportunity to join a growing and evolving business and be an integral part of this. You will be required to deliver effective communications aligned to corporate and business objectives, with a focus on internal communications. Your daily duties will include but not be limited to:  Driving and supporting the internal employee brand and business strategy  Fostering a culture of good internal and external communication throughout the business  Delivering, maintaining and communicating crisis communications plans  Establishing and maintaining strong relationships with key stakeholders to understand business objectives and requirements and assist in the creation and delivery of effective communications  Engaging with all members of the Corporate Communications team to align activities and drive best value  Establishing and maintaining metrics for measurement of internal communications activity  Monitoring and report on efficancy of all activity to ensure value for money You will be required to devise and implement the internal communications strategy using a broad mix of channels, including a strong focus on the intranet to maintain consistent messaging. The role will involve working closely with the wider communications team and engaging with senior stakeholders across the business to ensure coherence and alignment between internal and external messages. The successful candidate will be able to demonstrate a solid background of communications at a senior level, ideally gained within the insurance of professional services sector. This experienced will include experience of media relations within a corporate environment, excellent copywriting skills, strong interpersonal skills to build relationships across all levels of the organisation and with external agencies, crisis communication management and experience of managing press relations. You will be rewarded a competitive salary, excellent benefits and the opportunity to work in a culture that will help you grow, develop and shape the role.
