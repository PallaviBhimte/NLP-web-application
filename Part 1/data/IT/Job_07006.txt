Title: Data Solutions Architect
Webindex: 69093049
Company: MCS Group
Description: MCS Group is working on a senior level role for a technology enthusiast who has the ambition to lead and build technical solutions within a sophisticated global enterprise level data environment. This senior role fits within a highly regarded team which is responsible for providing authoritative sources of reference data to internal clients globally through implementing state of the art technologies which promote the collection, storage, analysis, and distribution of product, pricing, account and corporate action data. This team is tasked with deploying solutions to increase competitiveness, through restructuring business processes, simplifying information access, and providing business intelligence around critical data. This is a senior role with broad responsibility for architecture and design process for programmes of work in the products domain. The successful candidate will have demonstrable expertise in enterprise systems architecture and design as well as a strong track record of systems delivery, from initial design to production readiness. You should be experienced in evaluating existing architecture and propose improvements. This is a handson role but operating at senior level there will be a responsibility to lead and mentor others within the team. Due to the nature of this role, the successful candidate will be expected to have a very broad expertise in multiple technologies and systems, in order to ensure the most appropriate design for systems to be implemented. Some minimum knowledge requirements will include Java  core enterprise (J****EE), Databases such as Oracle Sybase, Application Servers such as WebLogic as well as experience in Unix Linux preferably. Experience in Data Warehousing and specifically Ab Initio will also be desirable.
