Title: MI Operations Analyst
Webindex: 68178584
Company: IPS Group Ltd
Description: Suited to a motivated self starter, the MIdriven role will analyse complex data, motivate others into sharing information and deal with an array of stakeholders. Reporting to the Senior Operations Manager of this lead player in the London market, you will be using both management information and operations skills to ensure the department runs efficiently. Responsibilities include developing and delivering MI including reporting to various bodies and steering groups, taking notes during committee meetings and coordinating, analysing, interpreting information alongside colleagues on all levels. You will ensure information sharing to produce Operations Reports, provide executive support through speech and presentation preparation and improve processes wherever possible. Those with insurance and either operations or management information experience are welcome to apply.
