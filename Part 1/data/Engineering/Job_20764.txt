Title: Manufacturing Engineer  CNC Manufacturing

Description: Our client a specialist manufacturing group are looking to further strengthen their engineering process department. Awareness of Lean principles and an understanding of ISO 9000 and TQM, including experience of using software to generate N/C data combined with an understanding of machine tools. The successful candidate will therefore have a proven track record within modern manufacturing environment with the ability to lead from the front when required. In return you can expect ongoing development in this challenging and rewarding position.
