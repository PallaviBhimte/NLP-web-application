Title: Counselor
Webindex: 71737879
Company: Community Assessment Treatment Services, Inc.
Description: We are looking for a Counselor to join our Men's Residential Program Counseling Team. You will be providing direct, intensive treatment to our population of mental health, chemically dependent men, all of whom have had recent experience with the criminal justice system in one way or another. We are seeking a very strong mental health counselor to fill this position. In this position you will be required to: Conduct CIAIC assessment interviews with new clients and prepares psychosocial histories Develop initial and individualized treatment plans which address client presenting problems Monitors, evaluates and documents client progress toward attaining treatment goals, and revises treatment plans as needed to enhance client outcomes Maintain case documentation in confidential files Provides individual and group counseling services to clients and their families Prepares client discharge summaries and aftercare plans Meet with potential clients at Pickaway Correctional Facility Requirements: Broad clinical/problem assessment skills with specialization in alcohol and other drug assessment and intervention Specific knowledge of mental health and cooccurring conditions Knowledge of TC principles preferable, but not required Knowledge of individual counseling and group facilitation techniques Crisis intervention skills Knowledge of communitybased resources and accessing insurance benefits Excellent written and verbal communications skills Mandatory Computer literacy Knowledge of statistical tracking and reporting methods LSW or LPC with Chemical Dependency focus or an LCDC II, III or LICDC Since **** Community Assessment has been able to assist thousands of people with chemical dependency and mental health issues through assessment, crisis intervention, individual and group counseling, case management, intensive outpatient services, and residential treatment.
