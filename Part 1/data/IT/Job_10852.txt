Description: We are currently recruiting for a temporary IT Support Analyst Duties will include: To assist users in the diagnosis/resolution of IT problems. To maintain communication with during this time. If/where necessary, to liaise with suppliers and third line support to resolve specific support incidents. Skills required: Previous IT Support experience Good communication Have a good understanding and working knowledge of technologies such as SQL, Exchange Server, Active Directory, DNS and TCP/IP To have experience of working in, and Supporting: MS Windows XP, Windows Server 2003, MS Office Applications, Network and Local Printing, PC Hardware.
