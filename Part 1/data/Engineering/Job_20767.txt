Description: Job Elements:   We have some exciting opportunities for high calibre Technical Operators who are looking for new challenges and wish to continue their professional development in a dynamic and varied arena. The operator will be responsible for adhering to required standards for GMP batch manufacture and the understanding and accurate documentation of work including the manufacturing batch records.  The job holder will be required to work on their own initiative and largely independently The ideal candidate will have:  Candidates should be degree educated or have at least 5 years' experience within the Pharmaceutical Development/Manufacturing area  Have undergone training in Root Cause Analysis techniques and possess a working knowledge & understanding of Lean Manufacturing methodologies.  Candidates should also possess excellent written & verbal communication skills and be passionate about manufacturing best practice.
