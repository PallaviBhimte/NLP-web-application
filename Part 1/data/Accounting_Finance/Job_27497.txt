The Technical Reporting Team is responsible for preparing the statutory financial information for the Santander UK and Abbey National Treasury Services groups. This includes the UK statutory Annual Report and Accounts, the US Annual Return on Form ****F, and the halfyearly financial reports. The Team is also responsible for advising entity accountants on the preparation of individual financial statements for subsidiaries and other Santander businesses in the UK. The Technical Reporting Team is also responsible for ensuring that all Santander companies in the UK comply with International Financial Reporting Standards ( IFRS ) and, where applicable, the US SEC Regulations. This includes monitoring IFRS and US SEC technical accounting pronouncements, assessing their impact on Santander UK, drafting technical accounting guidance for the entity accountants, updating the Group Accounting Manual, and communicating this to stakeholders within Santander UK. The Team also develops and presents technical accounting training courses for Finance staff. The Technical Reporting Team works closely with Group Reporting, Management Information, Tax, and Legal experience in global banking and more branches worldwide than any other international bank.
