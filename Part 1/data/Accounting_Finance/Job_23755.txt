Title: Stock Accountant

Company: Lorus Consulting
Description: Lorus Consulting are currently recruiting a Stock Accountant for a growing, entrepreneurial retailer with overseas operations. In view of substantial growth objectives, our client wishes to strengthen the team, placing an emphasis on visibility and control of stock position and margin. The role will report directly to the Finance Director and encompass the following responsibilities:  Implement robust stock control procedures and drive continual improvements.  Manage the rolling stock take variances.  Analysis of sales and margin including the calculation of margin and checking of standard costs.  Monthly stock reporting and analysis for the business.  Support stock projects / initiatives. The ideal candidate will have significant stock exposure and demonstrate making continual improvements to processes. Additionally they are likely to hold a recognised accountancy qualification (ACA, ACCA, CIMA).
