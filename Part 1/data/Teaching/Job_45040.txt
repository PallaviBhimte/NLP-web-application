Description: School Examination Officer An Exams Officer is sought by a secondary school in Bury, Greater Manchester starting ASAP to cover long term leave. As Exams Officer you will have a range of duties that will include management of examination teams, including invigilators, and arranging examination timetables. You will also be responsible for organising relevant exams, ensuring all students are entered for the correct paper at the correct time, managing absentees and overseeing the issuing of examination papers. You will also send out papers to examiners, and subsequently handle the management and reporting of examination results. You will cover the use of SIMS for producing various documents such as seating plans, registers, and candidate statements of entry. To be considered as an Exam Officer you must have: Experience in the use of SIMS, Exams and Assessment modules and Microsoft Excel. Accurate typing and data entry skills. Excellent communication and correspondence skills to liaise with external organisations, senior leadership teams, parents and pupils. Excellent analytical skills. Experience providing an analysis of examination results. Organisational skills to manage all elements of schools examination resources.
