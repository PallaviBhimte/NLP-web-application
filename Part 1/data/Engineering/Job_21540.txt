Title: Internal Sales/Order Management Executive

Description: This role is an internal account manager. You will need experience in order processing, dealing with quotations for customers both over the phone and by email. The majority of the communication is done via email. You will be expected to seek opportunities to up sell and follow up on leads as and when necessary. There is no cold calling in the role but you will need to have self motivation to increase orders by upselling and providing an excellent service that satisfy's the customer enough to return to you for their next order. Due to the complexity of the orders you must have good attention to detail. This role would suit someone with an administrative background within sales. You must have an order processing background.
