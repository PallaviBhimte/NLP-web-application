Description: Collections Representative Central London up to **** per annum Our client, a retail banking organisation has an exciting opportunity for someone to move into a role where they can make a big impact in recovering monies owed to the Bank across all lending products, Retail and Commercial. This role will give individuals a unique opportunity to help set up, shape and grow a new and expanding department whilst learning the fundamentals of Collections activities. The jobholder will ensure the efficient and effective collection of arrears across all lending products. They will also be responsible for ensuring all customers are contacted in an effective and timely manner, and that all efforts are made to recover outstanding funds. A significant part of the role will require speaking to Customers and making arrangements to bring their accounts up to date. You will also send letters and emails to Customers. Typical responsibilities will include but not be limited to:  Manage collections cases from initial arrears to resolution  Engage with customers to discuss their indebtedness and intention make repayments  Build rapport and establishing a good customer relationship  Obtain Income and Expenditure details to gain a good understanding of the customers financial position and ability to repay borrowing  Negotiate repayments to rectify account position  Liaise with outsourced debt management company and third party debt management companies  Manage account closures/debt write off  Develop and maintain Collections processes and procedures  Ensure compliance to applicable regulations and statutory requirements  Respond in a timely and professional manner to internal and external customer queries The successful candidate does not need specific collections experience, but must have the following skills:  An effective communicator  Assertive and confident  Strong written communication skills  Ability to manage multiple tasks  Attention to detail  Numerate  Good organisational skills  Ability to work well with others and be a teamplayer  Previous experience in a collections role, ideally from a banking background Candidates with the skills outlined should apply with a full CV Randstad Financial Professional encourage applications from individuals of all ages backgrounds. Appointment will be made on merit alone but candidates must be able to demonstrate their ability to work in the UK.
