Title: Investments and Treasury Manager
Webindex: 70574244
Company: Harrison Holgate
Description: Reporting to the CFO the successful applicant will maintain the groups treasury and investment processes and relevant risk policies, support relationships between the external investment management companies, the CFO and the Investment Committee, manage asset allocations to ensure a balanced portfolio of investments with appropriate target returns, management of the groups liquidity policy and managing the exposure to foreign currencies. Suitable applicants must have a relevant professional qualification (Accountancy, Investments, and Treasury etc.) good technical knowledge of asset classes, strong ability to analyse market data, working knowledge of liquidity and foreign currency exposure management and working knowledge of UK/US GAAP and solvency II requirements on investment and treasury activities.
