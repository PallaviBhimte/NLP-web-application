Title: Laboratory / Site Chemist
Webindex: 66160446
Company: Matchtech Group plc.
Description: Introduction Excellent opportunity to work with a leading provider of sustainable environmental solutions, principally for the oil and gas industry. Important Background in chemistry required for this role. The Job My client works on the decontamination, disposal and management of NORM (Naturally Occurring Radioactive Material) for the offshore oil and gas producers as well as service providers. The main part of this role will be the analysis of this material by Gamma Spectroscopy, hydrocarbon content analysis, monitoring of air, soil and site effluent quality. The successful candidate would also be responsible for all the associated Quality Assurance systems. Experience in working with Radioactive material and waste water systems would be advantageous but not essential as training will be provided. To apply for this position, candidates must be eligible to live and work in the UK.
