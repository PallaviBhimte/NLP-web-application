Title: Microbiology Analyst/Technician
Webindex: 69011669
Company: SRG Science
Description: An established environmental contract testing organisation are seeking Microbiology Analysts/Technicians to join the team at their new Microbiology testing facility in Belfast. In this position, you will be responsible for the preparation and microbial analysis of environmental samples to UKAS standards. Key Responsibilities will include: Sample reception and prepation for analysis. Undertaking microbial analysis on environmental water samples. Entry of all results into the LIMS system, generating accurate data in a timely fashion. Developing and validating microbiological analysis methods. Ensuring all laboratory equipment and instrumentation is maintained in a clean and servicable condition. Monitoring and maintaining levels of all stocks and supplies. Solving basic problems with laboratory testing methods and consulting with the laboratory manager to ensure all requirements are met. The ideal candidate will be educated to degree level or equivalent in Microbiology or a similar subject. Previous experience working within the contract testing industry to UKAS standards would be a significant advantage however this is not essential. The ideal individual should be motivated and flexible with excellent communication skills. The ability to work within a small team is essential. The recruiter has stated that all applicants for this job should be able to prove they are legally entitled to work in the UK.
