Company: Kent Social Care Professionals
Description: Our client supports young people leaving care, typically **** to **** year olds. Service users include young people, youth offenders, plus young people with additional needs aspergers, autism, young mums. A Qualified Social Worker is required with at least 2 years employment experience with children / young people. You will be proactive and dynamic  with new ideas and ways to update processes. The role will involve leading our young people's side of the company  but also offer some support to the family side. Duties will include: Court visits, planning of staff – new placements/referrals, read/cross reference monthly reports, policies and procedures – modifying / updating, assisting with tender processes, supervision and staff appraisals of support workers, assessments – risk, family, child protection, set up and implement care plans, provide training opportunities for Support Workers  alongside other team leader, liaise with Care Managers, Family and Friends of young people, MultiDisciplinary Team (alongside Team Leader), represent Company at meetings. You will also lead practice on support work, attend meetings / reviews, claim backs, crisis situation, monitor Team Leaders whilst in the field, take the emergency phone on a rota basis, along with other senior members of staff. You will be flexible to meet the needs of the service. Please only apply if you are a current qualified Social Worker, with at least 2 years experience in a similar role working with children/young people. Our client is an Equal Opportunities Employer.
