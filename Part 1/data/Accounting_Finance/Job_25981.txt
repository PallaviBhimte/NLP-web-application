Title: Finance /Operations Administrator
Webindex: 68995687
Company: Copperfield Recruitment
Description: The purpose of the Finance Administrator is to provide full administrative support within the Finance area of the Service Centre. This is a team based role within a Shared Service Centre. Responsibilities and key activities Upload all quotations relating to chargeable works onto the customers own in house systems. Manage the quotation approval process for all National Account Customers via email communication. Raise dummy invoices against Purchase Orders for a major retail customer. Manage and maintain the job costing process for a major retail customer. Raise and process Invoices for all quoted repair works. Manage and maintain the SAP DMR in preparation for invoice processing. Manage debt management process so as to meet SLA s set. Collate details of all chargeable works ready for consolidated monthly payment application to the respective National Account Customers. Reconcile BACS payments against Invoices for correct allocation. Respond to Adhoc Finance queries. Manage and maintain the ongoing open quote report. Manage and maintain the rental accounts if applicable. Einvoicing Professional requirements Ability to work on computers. Basic Outlook and MS office skills. Basic SAP understanding is an advantage as is previous invoicing experience in a busy environment. Educated at GCSE Standard in both English and Mathematics. Good written and oral English standard. NVQ Level 2/**** in business administration or equivalent. PLEASE ONLY APPLY IF YOU LIVE WITHIN A COMMUTABLE DISTANCE OF THIS LOCATION.
