Title: Accounts Payable (AP) Clerk
Webindex: 70758282
Company: Lend Lease
Description: At Lend Lease, we work with communities, clients and our colleagues to create positive legacies from funding a project right through to developing and constructing state of the art buildings and infrastructure, including hospitals, roads and bridges. We also create vibrant residential communities, productive workplaces and retail destinations. We are committed to outstanding performance, which means supporting and nurturing our people wherever possible. The Accounts Payable (AP) Clerk will report to the Accounts Payable Manager and will assist in ensuring that all Supplier invoices are validated in accordance with the set controls, for input on COINS and Oracle as appropriate. The Job Holder will ensure the timeliness of validation to prevent back logs and will aim for full input accuracy at all times. This will involve ensuring that the volume of invoice batches is systematically managed for scanning and input within the team on a daily basis. The job holder will ensure all queries are managed in a timely and responsive manner. We are seeking candidates who have exposure to an ERP solution (Oracle and/or COINS preferred) and who have worked as an Accounts Payable Clerk previously, ideally within a Shared Services Department.
