Title: Media Payables Clerk

Description: A large PR and Advertising company located in Central London is currently looking to recruit a Media Payables clerk to join their large, sociable team. The key responsibilities in this position include:  matching, batching and coding invoices into DDS  preparing payment runs  processing staff expenses  liaising with suppliers to resolve outstanding queries  assisting with close of the ledger for month end  monthly reporting where appropriate The successful candidate for this role must be an experienced media payables clerk. You should be switched on, friendly and sociable with a strong work ethic. The team enjoys socialising outside of work but maintain a busy and professional atmosphere whilst in the office. If you are looking for a fun work environment, a great way to meet new people and make friends and a rewarding career that will lead to further opportunities this is an excellent role for you.
