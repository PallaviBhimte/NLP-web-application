Description: Jobs for nurses interested in research in London Research Nurses  UK Acute Coronary Syndrome Research Study Nurses urgently required to join this exciting study Hays have ten vacancies for band 5 level RGN's, who have experience in research are or are keen to get involved in this very interesting and satisfying work. A new cohort study investigating new diagnostic and predictive markers in Acute Coronary Syndromes, begins in January 20****3. The study is collecting baseline health information and samples from people presenting to A&E departments in West London. Participants will then be followed at regular intervals for several years, to determine final diagnosis and cardiac outcomes. The post calls for a currently NMC Registered General Nurse, with at least 1 year post registration experience, a flexible attitude and excellent interpersonal skills. Training will be given in taking standardised measurements. The ability to work as part of a team and an interest in research is essential. Previous research experience and ECG skills would be an advantage but is not essential as training will be provided. Other attributes such as venepuncture, accurate data management and computer literacy are also essential. Short Interviews are taking place at St.Mary's Hospital, Paddington on Wednesday 2nd January. Further interview dates are a possiblity, but the intended start date of the training for the study is 14th January.
