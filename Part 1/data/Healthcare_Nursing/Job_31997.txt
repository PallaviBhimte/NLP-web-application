Description: Qualified Social Worker  Adults  Merseyside A Qualified Social Worker is required to join an Adults Team based in the Merseyside area. Working within the Adults Team, this Qualified Social Worker will have the following responsibilities:  To manage a caseload which includes vulnerable users and / or those with complex problems, taking action where necessary to protect the users and others in emergency situations.  To make appropriate assessments of individuals social care needs, within specified time limits and in collaboration with health and other relevant agencies, taking into consideration the wishes of individuals and their carers.  To plan relevant responses to meet individual needs in collaboration with relatives, carers, advocates and representatives, and in consultation with health and other agencies.  To secure the delivery, within the financial resources available, of services to meet assessed needs.  To maintain an effective system for coordinating, monitoring and reviewing care plans and service standards; to identify gaps in services and to advise the Line Manager accordingly. The successful Qualified Social Worker will be GSCC registered, have a current CRB and 2 years post qualification experience.
