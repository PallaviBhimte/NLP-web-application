Title: Treasury Associate
Webindex: 67102425
Company: Hays Senior Finance
Description: A multinational leisure business requires a Treasury Assistant to join their team in London. Responsible for treasury operations across the EMEA region, this is a job that will provide a technical challenge and an opportunity to develop your treasury skills in a fast paced environment. Working alongside the Treasury Analyst, your primary responsibility will be to monitor the daily cash requirements for the business and it's subsidiaries. You will be required to update the daily EU balances, maintain the bank account database (managing Excel spreadsheets, documentation and the opening and closing of accounts). In addition to this you will produce and verify FX confirmations and direct debit mandates. This job has significant potential for growth, there will be an opportunity to take on more responsibility from the Treasury Analyst and you will also be involved in a major project currently being undertaken within the team. You will be a driven, enthusiastic individual and possess an ability to communicate with credibility. This will be in addition to having good attention to detail and intermediate/advanced Excel skills (Pivot Tables, Formulae).
