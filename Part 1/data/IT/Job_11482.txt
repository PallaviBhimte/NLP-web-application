Title: Finance Support Officer  Leasehold

Description: The main purpose of this role is to assist with the preparation and rendering of actual service charge accounts for each individual leaseholder, dealing with enquiries about invoices raised and associated matter, assisting with the recovery of service charges and administering payment schemes such as right to a loan, direct debits etc. The successful candidate will need particular experience in leasehold service charge calculations, presale enquiry and ideally right to buy processing experience.
