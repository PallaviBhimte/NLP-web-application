SPECIFIC RESPONSIBILITIES Continuous Improvement of assembly & test processes, including potential for partial or full automation. Improve & maintain productivity in manufacturing cells. Complete thorough problem solving, root cause analysis & decision making with robust corrective actions. Application of Lean manufacturing, PFMEA, Six Sigma and other engineering tools. INTERPERSONAL SKILLS Work with Integrity and urgency. Able to own and coordinate engineering projects. Able to prioritise and take appropriate action (H & S, Warranty, Productivity). Able to build relationships at all levels within Production, Engineering, NPD and Purchasing. Operate without need for technical supervision. Coach / Mentor / Utilise other engineers. ESSENTIAL EDUCATION AND EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS Degree or equivalent qualification in a relevant engineering subject or Equivalent engineering experience with a completed relevant engineering apprenticeship. Be able to demonstrate a sense of urgency in dealing with issues. Possess the ability to make good decisions under pressure. Able to demonstrate a history of continuous improvement in previous roles. Experience of communicating information at all levels across various functions: written & verbally, concisely and accurately. A tenacious, enthusiastic, results driven individual with excellent time management skills. Must be structured and organised with the ability to work alone, within a team or leading a team as required.
