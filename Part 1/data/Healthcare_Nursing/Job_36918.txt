Title: Mental Health Senior Social Worker
Webindex: 71335722
Company: MOD PPPA Recruitment
Description: Applicants must be qualified Mental Health Social Workers who are currently registered with the General Social Care Council. Required to work as a Mental Health Senior Social Worker (SSW) as a member of a Department of Community Mental Health (DCMH) multidisciplinary team (MDT). To provide safe, effective and professional mental health social work support to military patients, their families and the Chain of Command. The geographic area covered is extensive with significant travel with some overnight stays. A full driving licence is required, along with a flexible approach to ensure work is carried out, including attendance at meetings elsewhere in the UK and cover of other areas as required. If you wish to apply for this vacancy or would like further information then please visit the CS Jobs website.
