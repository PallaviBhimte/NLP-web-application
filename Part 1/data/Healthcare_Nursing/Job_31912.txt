Description: Laboratory Associate  Immunobiology ****  ****  Benefits Are you an immunologist or cell biologist looking for a job with great career prospects? Would you like to work for an innovative and expanding biotech company in London? Reporting to the Research and Development manager and working in a small team based state of the art laboratories in London; you will be responsible for the characterisation of blood samples and immune reconstitution assays on patient samples. This will involve the development and validation of antigen specific T cells and immuno assays. Specific Responsibilities will include;  In vitro culture of human peripheral blood cells and flow cytometric analysis  Isolation and functional analysis of lymphocytes from donors and patients  Compliance activities within the laboratory where required, including process and assay development  Reviewing and amending/drafting standard operating procedures for analytical equipment and analytical procedures To be considered for this role you will need;  A BSc or MSc in biological science (or equivalent experience), with a focus on immunology or cell biology  Experience of working with cellular products including cell culture and flow cytometry  Ability to multitask and prioritise multiple activities, ensuring efficient completion to appropriate standards  A high level of self motivation, self organization and attention to detail Experience with the following techniques would be highly beneficial, handling and characterisation of antigen specific T cells and immuno assay development, experience with flow cytometers including optical plate readers, ELISPOT reader, PCR/gene amplifiers, DNA gel running equipment and all other equipment related to the preparation and analysis of antigen specific T cells.
