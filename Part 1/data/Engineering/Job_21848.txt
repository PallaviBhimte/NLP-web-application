Title: M E Project Engineer (Water)
Webindex: 72427193
Company: Calco Services Limited
Description: Opportunities for Project Engineers to run a variety of M E supply and install contracts. Project Engineers with experience of project completion within the water industry and a minimum of HND / HNC Level will be strongly considered. Calco apologise that we cannot respond to all applicants personally and only those who match the criteria of the client will be contacted. Calco Services operates as both an employment business and agency.
