Title: Finance Reporting Analyst

Description: Finance Reporting Analyst is required for a global investment organisation who are currently experiencing extensive growth across their market. Due to this expansion a newly created role has arisen. The position reports into the Finance Operations and Change Manager and has the following responsibilities: To build and maintain an effective relationship with the investment portfolio manager. Analysing financial information relating to the investment portfolio. Liaise with auditors in relation to month end, QMA and the year end investment reporting. To support the Finance Operations Manager in team activities and projects as required. Deputise for and support the Treasury Accountant. The successful candidate will have had prior experience to investment accounting and will possess a strong monthly / quarterly reporting track record. You will have excellent academics and ideally be a Qualified Accountant. An aptitude for working in a change environment is essential.
