Webindex: 69537152

Description: Capita Employee Benefits are in search of a Pensions Analyst to join our team based in Blackfriars London. As a Pensions Analyst you will be responsible for working within the Data team, to interrogate results from Data tests, undertake further analysis of the pension scheme being audited and produce a client facing report to discuss failures and issues found together with suggested recommendations for resolution. You will be fully involved in the running, analysis, interrogation and production of client data reports and will be exposed to a lot of pension detail and will need to be able to assimilate the information quickly and correctly to draw various conclusions of the data issues found. The successful candidate will have the following:  Demonstrable experience in pension administration, both DB and DC and/or CARE  Strong analytical and problem solving skills.  Eye for detail and a high focus on quality and accuracy.  Ability to work without close supervision and work to deadlines.  Awareness of challenges facing pension schemes.  Capable of recommending changes to drive business objectives. Please note that due to this being a regulated area of our business, the successful candidate will be subject to various security checks.
