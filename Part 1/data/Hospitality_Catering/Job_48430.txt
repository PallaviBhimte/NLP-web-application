Title: Mobile Catering Trailer Development Worker
Webindex: 66744092
Company: Groundwork Bridgend & Neath & Port Talbot
Description: Responsible for the day to day operation of the Mobile Food Trailer. You will create an environment that is welcoming to existing and new customers and develop a selfsustaining community mobile food trailer that provides a varied menu together with consistent, professional service. You will provide an integrated service of catering provision, and supervision and training of paid staff and volunteers. A flexible approach to working hours is essential. Relevant catering qualification and relevant food and hygiene certificates or Equivalent Level of knowledge required. This post requires an Enhanced level of disclosure. The successful candidate will need to complete a CRB application for this level of disclosure.
