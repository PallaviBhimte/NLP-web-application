Title: Commercial Accountant
Webindex: 66643295
Company: IPS Finance Recruitment/Forsyt
Description: This is a business partnering role with an established and profitable company in the Skipton area, it is ideally suited to a finalist or newly qualified Accountant with good commercial acumen, strong communication skills and first rate Excel/financial modelling ability. The post holder will work extensively with non finance budget holders and manages within the business providing support in areas such as pricing, contract and new product analysis, cost of sale and margin analysis. This will entail ensuring that the management board understand income and costs within their division, the willingness and ability to challenge and to work in partnership with non finance people is essential to this role.
