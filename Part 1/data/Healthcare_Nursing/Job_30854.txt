Description: Qualified Occupational Therapist  North East Our client is looking for an experienced Qualified Occupational Therapist to join their Reablement Team in the North East. You will be involved in the assessment and care planning for people with a disability, with the emphasis on rehabilitation, promoting safety and maximising independence. To be eligible, you must possess a professional Diploma or Degree in Occupational Therapy and be registered with the Health Professions Council.
