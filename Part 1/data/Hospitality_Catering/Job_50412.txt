Title: Apprentice Bar Assistant
Webindex: 69551457
Company: NS Care Ltd
Description: This apprenticeship will be working in a local restaurant/bar. Your duties will be serving and presenting beverages, serving tables, stocking and ordering for the bar, keeping the area clean and tidy, changing kegs when required, general housekeeping of the bar area and welcoming customers. You will achieve an NVQ Level 2 in Hospitality Services on completion of your apprenticeship
