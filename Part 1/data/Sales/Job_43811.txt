Title: Negotiator  Banbury

Company: Selection Matters Ltd
Description: Key Responsibilities:  Ensure every customer is greeted in a friendly, positive and enthusiastic manner in an attempt to build rapport.  Ascertain applicants' needs by listening carefully to the details provided, probing where necessary to ensure all data acquired.  Qualify applicants, match them to suitable properties and organise viewings at the earliest opportunity.  Immediately advise suitable applicants of any new instructions, which may be suitable.  Ensure all viewings are promptly followed up and that the vendor is immediately advised of the details  Ensure that when an offer is accepted that all administrative duties are attended to and that the sale is actively progressed through to a satisfactory and business like conclusion.  Maintain full documentation of all applicants, their requirements and actions.  Ensure every effort is made to cross sell the clients products, in particular, on every occasion attempt to gain an introduction to our clients Home Conveyancing and Mortgage Services.  Optimise every opportunity to secure valuations and to obtain Instructions  Undertake valuations in a professional manner and in such a way that the vendor forms a positive and favourable impression of the clients ability to meet their needs. Skills Required:  Previous sales and/or customer service experience would be preferred.
