Description: Staff Nurses  RMN or RNLD We are currently seeking to recruit motivated and enthusiastic RMNs and RNLDs to the multidisciplinary healthcare team in various services for adults and adolescents. The successful candidates will need to be able to undertake and manage direct patient care, having participated fully in the assessment, planning and evaluation of care needs. In addition, you will need to have excellent communication skills, be a good team player and have outstanding clinical knowledge and skills.
