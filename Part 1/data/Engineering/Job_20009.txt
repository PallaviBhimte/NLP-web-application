Title: Extraction Process Engineer
Webindex: 70694022
Company: Thomas Telford  Ltd
Description: Our client processes natural and agricultural feedstocks to produce pigments, foods and fuels on a large site owned by a major multinational. The site uses a variety of large scale processes including evaporation, conditioning, flaking and pressing. The job holder will undertake production process trouble shooting, risk assessments and audits, the management of minor and medium sized engineering projects, designing and implementing process changes, reporting and action on HSEQ related faults, failures and nonconformities. The position would suit a chemical or mechanical engineer with experience of food processing or the extraction sector or extractive processes.
