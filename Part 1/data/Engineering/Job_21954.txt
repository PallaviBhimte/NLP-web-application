Description: Electrical Draughtsman/Technician, Stockton on Tees (Permanent role) My client is a world leader in design and manufacture of bespoke engineering products. They are currently looking to add to their already successful team and recruit an Electrical Draughtsman / Technician. The role will be based in both the design office and production facility. The suitable candidate will have had experience in design/development of electrical/electronic systems and experience in electrical/electronic system build. They should have experience in using drafting packages, ideally AutoCAD, to produce electrical diagrams. The suitable candidate should also be able to work on panel wiring and electrical assembly/sub assembly, be able to work from design schematics, layouts and be able to handle tight deadlines. Some Knowledge of PLCs (experience of Siemens and Rockwell) would be desirable but not essential. The suitable candidate will be given all the training necessary to become a competent person in the use of AutoCAD Electrical. This will allow the candidate to produce electrical circuit schematics, panel layouts and generation of BOM information, to create and upkeep/generation of component libraries. This will also aid the candidate in being able to take instruction and concept design and turn them into a complete working electrical documentation package suitable for submission to workshop personnel and inclusion into product manuals. Background Previous electrical/mechanical engineering experience within a manufacturing environment is essential. Good electromechanical assembly background. Practical experience of installation work, fault finding and testing together with an understanding of control circuits. Qualifications BTEC National Certificate/Advanced Level City Guilds or NVQ**** (or older equivalents) in electrical engineering with significant post training experience. 17th Edition Certification in IEE Wiring Regulations and Electrical Installation.
