Title: Utilities Engineer
Webindex: 70694038
Company: Thomas Telford  Ltd
Description: Our client is a major manufacturer of bespoke and generic pharmaceuticals operating an integrated API and secondary finishing plant that facilitates the production of complete medicines. This is a challenging role for a chemical of mechanical engineer keen to develop their career within GMP manufacturing. The job holder will assume responsibility for ensuring the maximum availability and reliability of utilities, for the identification and management of utility projects and for ensuring that utility systems remain compliant with Good Manufacturing Practise. Specific duties will include monitoring utility output, performance and trending through computerised data management and mining, providing technical guidance to the wider engineering teams, representing the function at audits, write and review URS s, SOP s and generate design documentation, undertake plant reliability and availability studies including root cause analysis, develop and execute utility projects authorised by the head of pharmaceutical engineering, etc.
