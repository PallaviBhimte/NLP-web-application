Description: Grafton Banks Finance is currently assisting a wellknown large Financial Services organisation in recruiting an experienced Divisional Financial Controller. You will also be tasked with understand the links between commercial accounts and forecasted budget and take responsibility in driving and developing an efficient and effective internal controls environment, whilst creating an excellent approach to risk management. The ideal candidate will be a qualified accountant (ACA, CIMA, ACCA) and have strong financial services experience. You will have strong communication skills and the ability to problem solve. The suitable candidate will be rewarded with a competitive salary and a rewarding career with the potential of progression.
