Title: Mixed Tax Associate
Webindex: 71566384
Company: Jam Recruitment Ltd
Description: An excellent opportunity has arisen for an ambitious Mixed Tax Associate to join one of the UK's leading Legal Firms to specialise in providing both Personal and Corporate Tax assistance to the current Mixed Tax Manager. As a Mixed Tax Associate you will be responsible for the management of a diverse client portfolio assisting both Corporate and Personal Clients with their tax affairs. You will be assisting the Mixed Tax Manager with the firm's client relationships from the initial set up as well as providing an excellent ongoing service. Whilst managing a portfolio you will be in control of reviewing and submitting tax returns, completing both personal and corporate tax computations as well as liaising with HMRC. You will be confident managing your own time and workload as well as working within an integral team. You will also be familiar with PAYE Settlement Agreements and VAT returns. In order to be considered for this role you will have acquired relevant experience in both Personal and Corporate Tax, confident in advising clients on both areas of tax. The ability to liaise with individuals at all areas of business is a large part of the role therefore good communication skills are essential. Ideally you will be ATT, CTA ACCA or ACA qualified however individuals studying with a strong educational background and extensive experience in Tax and or Accounting will be considered.
