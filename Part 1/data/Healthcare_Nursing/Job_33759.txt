Title: Professional Nurse Advisor
Webindex: 68718517
Company: NHS Greater Glasgow & Clyde
Description: You will provide expert professional leadership and direction for Community District Nurses, Public Health Nurses, School Nurses, and other nursing staff within Children's and Families, and Adult Services across East Renfrewshire and Inverclyde CHCPs .In addition you will provide specialist knowledge and expertise in the field of Public Health Nursing across all CH(C)Ps (non Glasgow city areas) and as such must hold a Public Health Nursing Qualification and relevant experience in this area of practice. This remit of the post includes governance of professional regulatory requirements and standards; practice development; professional advisory role to CHCPs Senior Management Teams; and professional lead for the strategic development of Health Visiting and School Nursing Services in non Glasgow CH(C)Ps. You will be line managed by the CHCP Director and professionally accountable to the Nurse Director  Partnerships. Applications are invited from Registered Nurses with Specialist Community Public Health Nurse Qualifications that are educated to Higher Degree level or equivalent and can provide demonstrable evidence of CPD with direct relevance to the responsibilities of the post.
