Chubb Systems which provides large integrated electronic security solutions to major organisations across the UK are seeking to recruit an Electrical Project Engineer based in the South who has the 17th Edition IEE wiring regulations qualification and previous experience in electrical installations/ electrical design gained in industrial sectors such as Water and Process, Industrial Production, Facilities Management, Oil Gas, Building Services, Rail, Power or Utilities. The objective of the role is to to ensure that Electrical and Technology Installation and Commissioning Works are deployed effectively, economically and in accordance with all appropriate regulations and specifications to allow the company to minimise risks and maximise profits on contracts. Key duties will entail assisting the design team with verification and validation of designs, ensuring electrical installation works are carried out to all current IEE 17th Edition electrical standards and in accordance with specifications defined by our clients and duly audit, providing electrical installation guidance and support to operational delivery teams and subcontractors, providing installation solutions that meet all the requirements of the programme, producing Electrical installation risk assessments and method statements (RAMS), management of all onsite electrical installation works undertaken internally or by our nominated subcontractors, producing progress reports, attending site integration meetings and producing and updating all electrical redline drawings for the whole of the electrical installation works including General Arrangement drawings,Single Line Diagrams, Block Cable Diagrams, Electrical loopdiagrams, Cable schedules, Instrumentation Schedules, Installation Test and Inspection Schedules/ checks and cable route diagrams You will possess excellent interpersonal skills, a positive ``can do`` attitude and must have completed an approved Electricians Apprenticeship, with previous experience in Electrical installations/ electrical design in industrial sectors and at least 5 years post apprenticeship `field` experience. Unfortunately this opportunity would not be suitable to Electrical Specialists whose previous experience has been within domestic or small scale commercial contracts We welcome applications from talented Project Engineers, Electrical Engineers, Electricians, Electrical Engineering Managers, Site Engineers and electrical Project Managers who possess the above criteria and wish to develop a career within UTC.
