Title: Senior Overhead Lines Designer
Webindex: 72238340
Company: Hays
Description: If you are a Senior Overhead Design Engineer with experience in the design and construction of HV or EHV Overhead Lines and you are looking for a new job in Derbyshire, then the following job could be of interest to you. Working for one the nation's engineering giants, you will be responsible for the complete design life cycle of high voltage overhead lines. Initially you will analyses contract documentation and liase with various parties to ascertain client needs. You will then design systems as defined by your earlier activities while analysing and resolving any design problems. Finally you will be involved on the testing process to verify the suitability of you designs. This may involve providing calculations or reports to justify test load and results as well as assisting in the analysis of text failures and performing redesigns if necessary. To be considered for this role you should have experience performing the above activities in HV and EHV OHL construction and design. As such you should be familiar with the relevant UK, European and International design codes. You may be from a civil, electrical or engineering educational background. Salary is commensurate with skill set and experience. If you have the above experience and want to take up the opportunity to work for a renounced business in a secure and busy environment and you would like to be considered for the role, then please apply online today. We are also looking for a standard OHL Design Engineer.
