Description: EMBEDDED SOFTWARE ENGINEER  VB / C  ****  ****  PERMANENT A leading engineering organisation in Andover is currently recruiting for an additional Embedded Software Engineer to join their controls team on a permanent basis. You will be responsible for developing code for PICs and microcontrollers in embedded C and VB to create a control system for complex machinery. Your role will involve not only the coding but the testing of the systems including writing test programs both for the test of products in manufacturing and also for the service engineers to use to diagnose faults on equipment. Additionally you will be involved in the design of storage systems for test information in database format therefore experience of working with SQL databases would be hugely advantageous. For the position of Software Engineer you should be educated to HND/degree level or equivalent in electronics, computer science or similar. It is essential that you have experience of programming in embedded C and preferably have knowledge of VB (visual basic), you will have worked previously with PIC or other microcontrollers. Any knowledge of Siemens PLC software would also be advantageous. The ideal candidate will have previous experience of developing control systems. This position offers a pension, life assurance, 20 days holiday The position is commutable from Winchester, Salisbury, Eastleigh, Basingstoke, Southampton, Newbury, Swindon, Hampshire, Wiltshire, M3 corridor.
