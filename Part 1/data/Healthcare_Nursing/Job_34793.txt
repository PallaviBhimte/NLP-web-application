What we expect from you You are a Registered Nurse with an ENB998 or City and Guilds **** qualification and valid NMC PIN Number. You care about clinical excellence. About the role The impact you will have on patients lives will go far beyond care giving them independence, choice and privacy. You will get to know them, and their families, enabling you to follow personal care plans that really are as unique as every individual. You will take the lead in delivering oral and IV chemotherapy and other cancer related therapies, educating patients and maintaining high quality care documentation. Every day will be different, you will make decisions, deliver care plans and have the flexibility to prioritise and manage your own workload. You will be confident in reporting all activity, risks and issues to your line manager. You will also be able to demonstrate your experience of cannulation. This role is covering the East Sussex and Kent areas. You will have an oncology or haematology background, a current NMC registration and hold a full EU driving licence.
