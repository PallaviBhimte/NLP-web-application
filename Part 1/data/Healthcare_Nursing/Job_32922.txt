Description: Beautiful nursing home registered for Dementia service users is recruiting for a Deputy Nursing Home Manager to join their established team. This is an excellent opportunity to join a supportive care group with a good reputation for quality of care. The role will involve all aspects of supporting the home manager including rotas, careplanning, staff supervision and recruitment, clinical management and stepping up to manage the home in the managers absence. To be considered for the role you must have worked with dementia service users previously, be a qualified nurse (RGN or RMN) and have some experience of staff supervision. You must have a person centred approach and have a real passion for dementia care.
