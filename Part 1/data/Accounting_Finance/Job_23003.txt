Description: This role involves working with the UK Monitoring Team to assist with the delivery of the annual risk based monitoring program. You will be responsible for conducting and reporting of specific monitoring and sample testing reviews.  Undertake allocated compliance monitoring tasks from within the annual monitoring programme.  Conduct and document monitoring reviews to assess compliance with and adherence to regulations and internal policies. Follow established global policies, procedures and minimum standards requirements for monitoring and sample testing activities.  Develop and enhance current test strategies employed to continually improve the monitoring framework and methodology.  Investigate and close out any issues identified thoroughly and in a timely manner. Ensure significant issues are escalated appropriately and promptly.  Work collaboratively with monitoring team colleagues and other compliance resource at all levels sharing business unit knowledge and promoting best practice across the function.  Conduct business unit and/or thematic reviews in accordance with the firm's risk framework, developing, identifying risks and assessing the mitigating control environment.  Record monitoring findings in a standard format within the Compliance Monitoring Tool in a timely manner to ensure all risk data is accurately and appropriately captured.
