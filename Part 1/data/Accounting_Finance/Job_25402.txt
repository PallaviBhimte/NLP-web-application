Description: Elevation Accountancy Finance are currently recruiting for a Finance Supervisor. Based in Doncaster, my client is a specialist services and materials supplier to a wide portfolio of clients across the construction, defence, utilities, manufacturing and marine industries. With modern working offices, onsite parking and a friendly working environment this is a rare and exciting opportunity within the Doncaster market. The successful candidate will be responsible for working with two Accounts Clerks in ensuring the smooth running of the Credit Control and Purchase Ledger functions for the company. This role will be the link between the Finance Director and the accounts team so will be the main point of contact for queries and problems that arise within the department and build strong working relationships with the companies customers and suppliers. Elevation Accountancy Finance would be keen to speak with candidates with the following skills and experiences:  Overall management of the credit control and purchase ledger functions  Chasing debt with key clients  Apply credit limits and conduct risk assessment for new customers  Meeting with suppliers and customers to build strong working relationships  Dealing with queries on both credit control and purchase ledger that come into the department  Working with high value/low volume invoices from suppliers (including international businesses)  Processing supplier payments Elevation Accountancy Finance is a specialist division of Elevation Recruitment focusing on the recruitment of talented Accountancy and Finance professionals across all levels, from Ledger Clerks through to Financial Directors in Yorkshire and surrounding regions.
