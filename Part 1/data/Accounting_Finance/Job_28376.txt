Title: Operational Due Diligence (ODD) Analyst
Webindex: 71356425
Company: Livingston Edwards Ltd
Description: Our client, a City based asset manager is looking for an Operational Due diligence analyst for its Hedge Fund business. The overall purpose of the role is to provide qualitative operational due diligence and evaluation of hedge funds: this includes carrying out, onsite and desk based ODD; background checks; liaising with administrators; monthly NAV sign off; fund audit oversight. The successful candidate needs to be able to understand the key operational procedures in the hedge fund industry, demonstrate knowledge of hedge fund strategies, excellent written and oral communication skills, knowledge of Fund NAV oversight and strong research skills and attention to detail. Applicants will ideally be degree educated and either currently working in hedge fund ODD or as an auditor within a hedge fund practice.
