Title: Project Manager  Nordic Region
Webindex: 68368737
Company: ESP RECRUITMENT
Description: A leading destination management company seeks a Swedish or Finnish speaking Project Manager to join their Londonbased UK division. The focus of the role will be project management for incoming corporate incentive and conference groups into the UK, primarily from the Nordic region of Europe. Your main duties will involve dealing with client enquiries from point of inception to operation of the group programmes, including full account management. The ideal candidate will have gained experience either with a tour operator, event management agency or destination management company. You must be creative, an excellent communicator and a strong teamplayer, who is also confident and able to work independently and take on responsibility. Good organisational skills, attention to detail and knowledge of London and the UK are required. Applications will only be considered from candidates who are fluent in Swedish, Finnish or another Scandinavian language.
