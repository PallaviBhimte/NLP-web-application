Description: MCS Group is working with a growing company in Lisburn who have quickly established themselves as an employer of choice throughout the Technical and Software Engineering market. The company continue to grow throughout Europe and want their Lisburn HQ to play a key part in this. They have, over the last number of years, implemented an Agile software methodology throughout the company and are now in a position to add to the Product Owners department. These candidates will play a key role in the development and deployment of the next wave of systems for the company. Product owners will be expected to be able to handle a range of duties including • Documenting User stories (Requirements of the system and what the technology needs to do) • Liaising with Product managers/Sales teams and Third parties to ensure everyone is getting what they need • Ensuring the Developers have all the necessary information to build the required product • Continually review and update the teams priorities as the market changes and understanding becomes clearer. To be successful you will need to be an outstanding communicator and negotiator with people you do not manage. You should have good software knowledge to understand technical constraints and have a good understanding of your commercial market.
