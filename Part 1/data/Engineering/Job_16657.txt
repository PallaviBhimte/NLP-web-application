Title: Technical Planners (Repairs Maintenance  Water)
Webindex: 68591334
Company: Calco Services Limited
Description: Reporting to the planning manager as Technical Planner you will be responsible for planning of R M works, including applying for permits, TMA, suspensions and extensions. You will also be responsible for planning emergency works and ensuring they are dispatched on time for the gangs to complete as well as the following responsibilities:  Prioritise and plan works in the backlog.  Attend to emergency works ensuring all notice and streetworks compliance is in place.  Ensure all jobs are planned correctly and investigated thoroughly.  Conduct all planning in compliance with streetworks legislation.  Ability to work on own initiative and hit daily targets for planned works.  Ensure all permits are correctly applied for.  Investigate jobs that are not done and report accordingly.  Keep daily records of works planned and issues encountered.  Liaise closely with the Client to ensure works are competed in a timely manner and on the first hit.  Report daily to the line manager of outputs, problems, issues. Planning experience and London Streetworks knowledge or experience is required. Any water industry experience particularly with Thames Water would be beneficial but is not essential. You must be IT Literate with a good knowledge of Microsoft Word Excel.
