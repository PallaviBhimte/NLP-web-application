Description: A leading supplier of industrial measurement solutions requires a CMM Programmer / Operator to join their existing team of engineers. Growing market share has led to a new site openning in Huddersfield creating excellent career opportunities for enthusiastic and professional engineers to join this developing and expanding sector. The role of CMM Programmer / Operator will be to provide contract inspection services, applications programming, software and applications training to customers. You will be expected to deal directly with customers at management level, to work under your own initiative often to demanding deadlines and to communicate effectively with customers a a technical and commercial level. As an experienced CMM Programmer / Operator you will be able to demonstrate a high level of competence and experience in programming  Mitutoyo Cosmos CMM software would be highly advantageous although experience in other proprietary software will be considered. In addition a career history and relevant qualification in a mechanical engineering environment would be an advantage. The successful CMM Programmer / Operator will enjoy a competative salary, extensive ongoing training, and the opportunity to join a well established company that is flourishing in this growth sector. As a new site career progression is a very real opportunity for the right individuals.
