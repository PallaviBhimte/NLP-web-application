Description: Technical Planner Warwickshire  Negotiable Our client is a global leader in the Power Generation sector. They are currently seeking a Technical Planner to join their team at the Warwickshire based offices. As a Technical Planner you’ll be required to support tender and site planning functions and to update programmes during the project phase. The role: As Technical Planner you’ll produce Steam Turbine and Generator Outage programmes, from tender phase through to project phase where you will support the site management team by updating project programmes and incorporating emergent works. You’ll also implement continuous improvement programmes to ensure that they reflect market leading techniques and technology. You: You'll ideally have a HND in an Engineering discipline or will have the relevant experience and you’ll have a technical knowledge of large steam turbines and generators. You’ll have experience of planning software including Primavera P**** Suretrak, Microsoft Project and Excel. Training would be available to improve on these skills where required. This role will require visits to various business sites and depending on location may involve overnight stays every so often.
