Description: MBA Student Services Manager MBA, Degree Programmes Office London Business School, Regent s Park, London Up to **** per annum (dependent on skills, knowledge and experience) plus excellent benefits Closing date: 4th February 2013 The position: To lead the programme management team in the operational planning and delivery of student services to the MBA Programme ensuring high satisfaction throughout the student journey. The Degree Programmes portfolio represents over 50% of London Business School s revenues. With our programmes consistently ranked in the top 5 in the world by the Financial Times, the department also plays an essential role in supporting the School s brand and reputation. You will possess excellent communication, customer service, interpersonal, project management, time management and presentation skills, show initiative and drive, and have the ability to negotiate and influence at a senior level whilst building and maintaining positive relationships.
