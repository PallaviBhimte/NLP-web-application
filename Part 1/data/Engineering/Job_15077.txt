Title: Planning Policy Officer
Webindex: 66127042
Company: Broads Authority
Description: This is an exciting opportunity to be involved in the management of the Norfolk and Suffolk Broads, an internationally important wetland with status equivalent to a national park The Planning Policy Officer is responsible for the preparation of all planning policy documents (LDF, Local Plan) including: Public consultations Public Examinations Conducting background research into policy and procedural issues Responding to consultation documents. The successful candidate will also prepare the Annual Monitoring Report, advise on planning policy issues and prepare and present reports to Committee. Applicants should have a degree or Diploma in Town and Country Planning, be a full member of the RTPI and have demonstrable experience working in planning policy. Good IT skills, including database management, plus excellent all round communication skills are also essential for this post.
