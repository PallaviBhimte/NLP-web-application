Title: Regional Domiciliary Care Manager
Webindex: 69590401
Company: Hays
Description: An excellent opportunity for an experienced Domiciliary Care Manager to join an outstanding provider of domiciliary and extra care to oversee several localities in their Cheshire region. As Services Team Manager (Registered), the post holder will be responsible for the delivery, development and performance of a number of integrated care services across several sites within the Cheshire region. Overseeing a number of localities, the key focus of this role will be to effectively lead and manage the locallybased care teams towards the required high standards of service delivery and customer satisfaction. There is also a strong emphasis on operational management within this role, with the delivery of strong financial performance pivotal to the region's ability to deliver commercial and indeed social objectives.The successful candidate will also be required to act as the local lead in developing and and sustaining effective working relationships in order to support the service delivery within the specified localities. The successful candidate will hold a Care Management qualification (NVQ Level 4 or equivalent) and be able to demonstrate a strong background in a similar post and environment. The ability to successfully lead a team of staff towards the achievement of varied objectives is essential for this role, as is the ability to 'lead from the front' in terms of care service delivery to ensure that the needs and requirements of service users are met at every opportunity.
